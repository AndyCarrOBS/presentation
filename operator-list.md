## Regional Statistics

### Central and Eastern Europe (CEE)
- **Pay-TV Households:** ~30 million across 7 key markets (Bulgaria, Czech Republic, Hungary, Moldova, Poland, Romania, Slovakia)
- **Additional Markets:** ~6 million pay-TV customers in Albania, Bosnia, Croatia, Montenegro, North Macedonia, Serbia, Slovenia

### Europe Overall
- **Smart TV Penetration:** 187 million European households equipped with Smart TVs (Q4 2024), surpassing pay-TV subscriptions
- **HbbTV Adoption:** >100 million households in Europe (2025), projected 50% penetration rate by 2026
- **SES Satellite Reach:** 175.8 million homes in Europe

## Notes

- This list focuses on major operators and may not be exhaustive
- Operator ownership and branding can change frequently due to mergers and acquisitions
- Some operators operate across multiple countries
- Free-to-air broadcasters typically operate multiple channels
- CI+ cam requirements vary by operator and service tier
- IPTV services typically use operator-provided STBs, while cable/satellite platforms may require CI+ cam for third-party TVs
- Subscriber numbers are approximate and may vary by reporting period
- Some operators do not publicly disclose subscriber data
- Market share data for broadcasters refers to viewership/audience share, not subscriber counts
