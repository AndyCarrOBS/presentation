# Turkey - Demographics & Market Data

## 1. Core Population & Household Structure

### Population Basics
- **Total population**: ~85 million (early 2024)
- **Urban vs rural**: ~76% urban, ~24% rural
- **Population density**: ~110 people/km²
- **Household count**: ~25 million households
- **Average household size**: ~3.4 people

### Age Structure
- **0-14**: ~22%
- **15-24**: ~16%
- **25-44**: ~30%
- **45-64**: ~22%
- **65+**: ~10%
- **Median age**: ~33 years

### Household Types
- **Single-person households**: ~12%
- **Family households with children**: ~65%
- **Multi-generational homes**: ~18%
- **Socioeconomic segmentation**: ABC1 ~40%, C2DE ~60%

## 2. Economic Indicators

### Macro-economics
- **GDP (total)**: ~€900 billion (2024)
- **GDP per capita**: ~€10,600
- **GDP growth rate**: ~4.5% (2024)
- **Inflation rate**: ~65% (2024)
- **Disposable income per household**: ~€12,000/year
- **Cost of living index**: 45 (EU average = 100)

### Consumer Spend Capacity
- **Average telecom spend per household**: ~€30/month
- **Average entertainment/media spend**: ~€40/month
- **Credit card penetration**: ~50%
- **Digital payments adoption**: ~60%

## 3. Connectivity & Device Penetration

### Broadband
- **Broadband penetration**: ~92% of households have broadband internet access (fixed or mobile, 2023), ~62% have fixed broadband at home, ~74-75 million internet users (~86-88% penetration)
- **Average broadband speed**: ~90 Mbps
- **FTTH availability**: ~40% (growing)
- **Mobile data penetration**: 4G/5G coverage ~95%
- **Mobile broadband ARPU**: ~€15/month
- **Internet usage**: Nearly universal among young and middle-aged (over 96% of people in twenties and thirties online), gender gap: 92.2% men vs 85.4% women (ages 16-74) are internet users, senior citizens (65-74): ~25% use e-government or online services (2024)

### Devices
- **Smart TV penetration**: ~48%
- **Streaming media players**: ~28%
- **Smartphone penetration**: ~88%
- **PC/tablet penetration**: ~72%
- **Console penetration**: ~22%

## 4. Television Access & Platform Penetration

### TV Platform Split
- **Total TV households**: ~25 million
- **Pay-TV households**: ~18 million (~72%)
- **Free-to-air households**: ~7 million (~28%)

### Market share by delivery type:
- **Satellite**: ~40%
- **Cable**: ~25%
- **IPTV/FTTH**: ~25%
- **DTT**: ~10%
- **OTT-only households**: ~8%

### Platform Technology & CA
- **CI+ penetration**: ~30% of pay-TV households
- **CAM vs STB share**: ~25% CAM, ~75% STB
- **Conditional Access systems**: Nagravision, Irdeto, Viaccess
- **Smartcard vs cardless CAS**: Smartcard dominant
- **Operator-owned STBs vs retail devices**: ~75% operator STBs

### OTT & Streaming
- **SVOD penetration**: ~35% of households
- **AVOD penetration**: ~30% of households
- **Hybrid STB penetration**: ~28%
- **TV Everywhere usage**: ~22% of pay-TV subscribers

## 5. Language, Culture & Content Consumption

### Language
- **Primary language**: Turkish
- **% bilingual population**: ~30% (English as second language)
- **Requirements**: Turkish dubbing/subtitling preferred

### Content Preferences
- **Top genres**: News, sports, movies, drama series, reality TV, Turkish soap operas
- **Local content importance**: Very high - Turkish content preferred
- **Sports rights**: Football (Süper Lig), Basketball, Volleyball
- **Public service broadcaster influence**: Strong (TRT)

## 6. Regulatory Environment

### Broadcast Regulation
- **Licensing authorities**: RTÜK (Radio and Television Supreme Council)
- **Spectrum allocation**: DTT, satellite (Türksat, Eutelsat)
- **Must-carry rules**: Yes - public channels must be carried
- **Local content quota**: Turkish content quotas apply

### Consumer & Platform Regulation
- **Anti-piracy environment**: Moderate enforcement
- **CAS/DRM compliance**: Mandatory for pay-TV
- **CI+ certification requirements**: Yes, via CI+ Consortium
- **Taxes on TV subscriptions**: VAT 20%
- **Data protection**: Local data protection laws

## 7. Pay-TV, OTT, and Advertising Market KPIs

### Pay-TV
- **Pay-TV ARPU**: ~€18-28/month
- **Pay-TV revenue growth rate**: ~4-5% annually
- **Number of major operators**: 4-5 (D-Smart, Digiturk, Turkcell TV+, Tivibu, Teledünya)
- **Churn rate**: ~16-18% annually

### OTT/SVOD
- **Market size**: ~€600 million
- **Revenue/ARPU**: ~€7-9/month per service
- **Top players**: Netflix, BluTV, PuhuTV, GAIN, Digiturk Play
- **Subscriber growth rates**: ~15-18% annually

### Advertising
- **TV advertising revenue**: ~€1.2 billion
- **AVOD/CTV ad spend**: ~€250 million
- **Growth forecasts**: ~5-6% annually
- **Programmatic adoption**: ~32%

## 8. Market Forecasts

- **Pay-TV household forecast (5-10 years)**: Growth (~2% annually)
- **OTT/SVOD penetration forecast**: Growth to ~52% by 2030
- **Broadband and FTTH growth**: FTTH to reach ~60% by 2030
- **Economic forecast**: Strong growth (~4.5% GDP annually)
- **Population forecast**: Growth to ~90 million by 2030

## 9. Competitive Landscape

### Major Operators

**D-Smart**
- Platform type: Satellite
- Subscriber numbers: Largest pay-TV operator
- Market share: ~30% pay-TV
- Technology: Smartcards, CI+ compatibility varies
- Features: Multi-channel packages

**Digiturk**
- Platform type: Satellite, IPTV
- Subscriber numbers: Growing
- Market share: ~25% pay-TV
- Technology: Smartcards, Digiturk Play OTT
- Features: 4K, sports rights, Digiturk Play

**Turkcell TV+**
- Platform type: IPTV, OTT
- Subscriber numbers: Growing
- Market share: ~20% pay-TV
- Technology: Operator STBs, Turkcell TV+ platform
- Features: 4K, multi-device streaming

**Tivibu**
- Platform type: IPTV, OTT
- Subscriber numbers: Growing
- Market share: ~15% pay-TV
- Technology: Operator STBs
- Features: 4K, Tivibu streaming

**Teledünya**
- Platform type: Cable
- Subscriber numbers: Growing
- Market share: ~10% pay-TV
- Technology: CI+ CAM modules
- Features: Triple-play bundles
