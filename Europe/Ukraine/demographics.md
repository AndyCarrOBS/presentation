# Ukraine - Demographics & Market Data

## 1. Core Population & Household Structure

### Population Basics
- **Total population**: ~38.3 million (beginning of 2025), significant displacement due to conflict
- **Urban vs rural**: ~70.4% urban, ~29.6% rural
- **Population density**: ~63 people/km²
- **Household count**: ~15 million households
- **Average household size**: ~2.6 people

### Age Structure
- **0-14**: ~15%
- **15-24**: ~12%
- **25-44**: ~28%
- **45-64**: ~27%
- **65+**: ~18%
- **Median age**: ~42 years

### Household Types
- **Single-person households**: ~26%
- **Family households with children**: ~48%
- **Multi-generational homes**: ~16%
- **Socioeconomic segmentation**: ABC1 ~30%, C2DE ~70%

## 2. Economic Indicators

### Macro-economics
- **GDP (total)**: ~€200 billion (2024, significantly impacted by conflict)
- **GDP per capita**: ~€5,200
- **GDP growth rate**: ~3.0% (2024, recovery)
- **Inflation rate**: ~12.0% (2024)
- **Disposable income per household**: ~€8,000/year
- **Cost of living index**: 30 (EU average = 100)

### Consumer Spend Capacity
- **Average telecom spend per household**: ~€22/month
- **Average entertainment/media spend**: ~€28/month
- **Credit card penetration**: ~35%
- **Digital payments adoption**: ~50%

## 3. Connectivity & Device Penetration

### Broadband
- **Broadband penetration**: ~82.4% internet penetration (beginning of 2025), ~31.5 million internet users
- **Average broadband speed**: ~83.81 Mbps fixed internet speed, ~31.23 Mbps mobile internet download speed
- **FTTH availability**: ~35% (growing, infrastructure rebuilding)
- **Mobile data penetration**: 4G/5G coverage ~94.1% of mobile connections (broadband mobile internet 3G, 4G, 5G)
- **Mobile broadband ARPU**: ~€10/month

### Devices
- **Smart TV penetration**: ~45%
- **Streaming media players**: ~22%
- **Smartphone penetration**: ~78%
- **PC/tablet penetration**: ~68%
- **Console penetration**: ~18%

## 4. Television Access & Platform Penetration

### TV Platform Split
- **Total TV households**: ~15 million
- **Pay-TV households**: ~9 million (~60%)
- **Free-to-air households**: ~6 million (~40%)

### Market share by delivery type:
- **Satellite**: ~35%
- **Cable**: ~30%
- **IPTV/FTTH**: ~20%
- **DTT**: ~15%
- **OTT-only households**: ~8%

### Platform Technology & CA
- **CI+ penetration**: ~30% of pay-TV households
- **CAM vs STB share**: ~25% CAM, ~75% STB
- **Conditional Access systems**: Nagravision, Irdeto, Viaccess
- **Smartcard vs cardless CAS**: Smartcard dominant
- **Operator-owned STBs vs retail devices**: ~75% operator STBs

### OTT & Streaming
- **SVOD penetration**: ~32% of households
- **AVOD penetration**: ~28% of households
- **Hybrid STB penetration**: ~25%
- **TV Everywhere usage**: ~20% of pay-TV subscribers
- **Social media usage**: YouTube ~21.6 million users, Facebook ~13.9 million, Instagram ~12 million, TikTok ~17 million

## 5. Language, Culture & Content Consumption

### Language
- **Primary language**: Ukrainian (official), Russian
- **% bilingual population**: ~60% (Russian as second language)
- **Requirements**: Ukrainian dubbing/subtitling preferred, Russian content also consumed

### Content Preferences
- **Top genres**: News, sports, movies, drama series, reality TV
- **Local content importance**: Very high - Ukrainian content preferred
- **Sports rights**: Football, Basketball, Boxing
- **Public service broadcaster influence**: Strong (Suspilne)

## 6. Regulatory Environment

### Broadcast Regulation
- **Licensing authorities**: National Council of Television and Radio Broadcasting
- **Spectrum allocation**: DTT, satellite (Eutelsat, SES)
- **Must-carry rules**: Yes - public channels must be carried
- **Local content quota**: Ukrainian content quotas apply

### Consumer & Platform Regulation
- **Anti-piracy environment**: Moderate enforcement
- **CAS/DRM compliance**: Mandatory for pay-TV
- **CI+ certification requirements**: Yes, via CI+ Consortium
- **Taxes on TV subscriptions**: VAT 20%
- **Data protection**: GDPR compliant

## 7. Pay-TV, OTT, and Advertising Market KPIs

### Pay-TV
- **Pay-TV ARPU**: ~€15-25/month
- **Pay-TV revenue growth rate**: ~3-4% annually (recovery)
- **Number of major operators**: 4-5 (Viasat Ukraine, Volia, Kyivstar TV, OLL.TV)
- **Churn rate**: ~18-20% annually

### OTT/SVOD
- **Market size**: ~€200 million
- **Revenue/ARPU**: ~€6-8/month per service
- **Top players**: Netflix, Megogo, Sweet.TV, OLL.TV
- **Subscriber growth rates**: ~15-18% annually

### Advertising
- **TV advertising revenue**: ~€300 million
- **AVOD/CTV ad spend**: ~€60 million
- **Growth forecasts**: ~4-5% annually (recovery)
- **Programmatic adoption**: ~28%

## 8. Market Forecasts

- **Pay-TV household forecast (5-10 years)**: Recovery and growth (~3% annually post-conflict)
- **OTT/SVOD penetration forecast**: Growth to ~50% by 2030
- **Broadband and FTTH growth**: FTTH to reach ~55% by 2030 (infrastructure rebuilding)
- **Economic forecast**: Recovery growth (~3.0% GDP annually post-conflict)
- **Population forecast**: Significant uncertainty due to conflict and displacement
