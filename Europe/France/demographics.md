# France - Demographics & Market Data

- **Urban vs rural**: ~80% urban, ~20% rural
- **Population density**: ~118 people/km²
- **Household count**: ~29.5 million households
- **Average household size**: ~2.3 people

### Age Structure
- **0-14**: ~18%
- **15-24**: ~12%
- **25-44**: ~25%
- **45-64**: ~25%
- **65+**: ~20%
- **Median age**: ~42 years

### Household Types
- **Single-person households**: ~36%
- **Family households with children**: ~28%
- **Multi-generational homes**: ~8%
- **Socioeconomic segmentation**: ABC1 ~45%, C2DE ~55%

- **GDP (total)**: ~€2.9 trillion (2024)
- **GDP per capita**: ~€42,000

- **Cost of living index**: 100 (EU average = 100)

### Consumer Spend Capacity

- **Digital payments adoption**: ~75%

## 5. Language, Culture & Content Consumption

### Language
- **Primary language**: French
- **% bilingual population**: ~40% (English as second language)
- **Requirements**: Dubbing preferred over subtitling for most content

### Content Preferences
- **Top genres**: News, movies, sports, documentaries, drama series
- **Local content importance**: High - French content quotas apply
- **Sports rights**: Football (Ligue 1), Rugby, Tennis (Roland Garros)
- **Public service broadcaster influence**: Strong (France Télévisions)

## 6. Regulatory Environment

### Broadcast Regulation
- **Licensing authorities**: CSA (Conseil supérieur de l'audiovisuel)
- **Spectrum allocation**: DTT (MUX1-8), satellite (Eutelsat, SES)
- **Must-carry rules**: Yes - public channels must be carried
- **Local content quota**: 60% European content, 40% French content

### Consumer & Platform Regulation
- **Anti-piracy environment**: Strong enforcement
- **CAS/DRM compliance**: Mandatory for pay-TV
- **CI+ certification requirements**: Yes, via CI+ Consortium
- **Taxes on TV subscriptions**: VAT 20%
- **Data protection**: GDPR compliant

## 7. Pay-TV, OTT, and Advertising Market KPIs

### Pay-TV
- **Pay-TV ARPU**: ~€35-45/month
