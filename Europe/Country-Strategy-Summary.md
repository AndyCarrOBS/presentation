## Netherlands

For **Netherlands** the population is **17.65 million**, with **8.1 million TV homes**. The public service broadcasters, and their OTT apps are **NPO (NPO Start)**, **BVN (Best of Vlaanderen en Nederland)**. In the market are the following TV platforms **Ziggo/UPC (~3.83 million subscribers)**, **KPN (~2.17 million subscribers)**, **M7 Canal Digitaal (~37,520 subscribers)**.

**Ziggo/UPC**
This operator uses **CI+, HbbTV, Nagravision/Irdeto CA**. The operator is **likely** to engage with Roku. Amazon Fire TV is **active** in Netherlands. Technical specifications are **available** - Ziggo supports CI+ 1.4, HbbTV 2.0.1, uses Nagravision and Irdeto conditional access systems, and provides operator STBs with hybrid capabilities.

**KPN**
This operator uses **HbbTV, operator STBs, OTT apps**. The operator is **likely** to engage with Roku. Amazon Fire TV is **active** in Netherlands. Technical specifications are **available** - KPN Interactive TV platform supports HbbTV, provides IPTV services with operator STBs, and offers OTT applications.

**M7 Canal Digitaal**
This operator uses **CI+, smartcards, Nagravision/Irdeto CA**. The operator is **unlikely** to engage with Roku (satellite-focused, small subscriber base). Amazon Fire TV is **active** in Netherlands. Technical specifications are **available** - M7 Canal Digitaal uses CI+ CAM modules, smartcard-based conditional access, and satellite delivery.

In **Netherlands** these retail partners are where TV's are purchased **MediaMarkt**, **Coolblue**, **BCC**, **Expert**, **Bol.com**.

## Summary Notes

### Technical Specification Availability
- Most operators have **available** technical specifications through public documentation, industry reports, and technical standards compliance
- CI+ specifications are standardized via CI+ Consortium
- HbbTV specifications are standardized via HbbTV Consortium
- Conditional Access systems (Nagravision, Irdeto, Conax, Viaccess) have published specifications

### Roku Engagement Likelihood
- **Likely**: IPTV operators, cable operators with modern platforms, public service broadcasters (must-carry rules)
- **Unlikely**: Satellite-focused operators (M7 platforms, Allente), premium content platforms (Sky, Canal+), small regional operators

### Amazon Fire TV Activity
- **Active** in all target markets - Amazon Fire TV has presence across all European markets

### Retail Partners
- **MediaMarkt** is present in most markets (Netherlands, Belgium, Austria, Czech Republic, Poland, Hungary, Slovakia, Sweden)
- **Local retailers** vary by market but typically include electronics chains, department stores, and online retailers
- **Online retailers** (Bol.com, Alza, eMAG, etc.) are increasingly important for TV sales
