# Ireland - Demographics & Market Data

## 1. Core Population & Household Structure

### Population Basics
- **Total population**: ~5.02 million (2024)
- **Urban vs rural**: ~63% urban, ~37% rural
- **Population density**: ~72 people/km²
- **Household count**: ~1.8 million households
- **Average household size**: ~2.8 people

### Age Structure
- **0-14**: ~20%
- **15-24**: ~13%
- **25-44**: ~28%
- **45-64**: ~25%
- **65+**: ~14%
- **Median age**: ~38.5 years

### Household Types
- **Single-person households**: ~25%
- **Family households with children**: ~45%
- **Multi-generational homes**: ~8%
- **Socioeconomic segmentation**: ABC1 ~45%, C2DE ~55%

## 2. Economic Indicators

### Macro-economics
- **GDP (total)**: ~€550 billion (2024)
- **GDP per capita**: ~€110,000
- **GDP growth rate**: ~3.5% (2024)
- **Inflation rate**: ~2.8% (2024)
- **Disposable income per household**: ~€50,000/year
- **Cost of living index**: 120 (EU average = 100)

### Consumer Spend Capacity
- **Average telecom spend per household**: ~€65/month
- **Average entertainment/media spend**: ~€90/month
- **Credit card penetration**: ~85%
- **Digital payments adoption**: ~80%

## 3. Connectivity & Device Penetration

### Broadband
- **Broadband penetration**: ~94% of households have internet access (2024), ~86% utilize fixed broadband connections
- **Average broadband speed**: ~140 Mbps
- **FTTH availability**: ~60% (growing)
- **Mobile data penetration**: 4G/5G coverage ~98%
- **Mobile broadband ARPU**: ~€28/month
- **Average household broadband consumption**: ~5,494 GB (2024), fiber broadband households: ~6,888 GB average

### Devices
- **Smart TV penetration**: ~72% (Northern Ireland reference, 2025)
- **Streaming media players**: ~50%
- **Smartphone penetration**: ~92%
- **PC/tablet penetration**: ~88%
- **Console penetration**: ~45%

## 4. Television Access & Platform Penetration

### TV Platform Split
- **Total TV households**: ~1.8 million
- **Pay-TV households**: ~1.2 million (~65%)
- **Free-to-air households**: ~600,000 (~35%)

### Market share by delivery type:
- **Satellite**: ~35%
- **Cable**: ~25%
- **IPTV/FTTH**: ~30%
- **DTT**: ~10%
- **OTT-only households**: ~15%

### Platform Technology & CA
- **CI+ penetration**: ~40% of pay-TV households
- **CAM vs STB share**: ~30% CAM, ~70% STB
- **Conditional Access systems**: NDS/Videoguard, Nagravision
- **Smartcard vs cardless CAS**: Mixed (smartcard dominant)
- **Operator-owned STBs vs retail devices**: ~75% operator STBs

### OTT & Streaming
- **SVOD penetration**: ~70% of households
- **AVOD penetration**: ~58% of households
- **Hybrid STB penetration**: ~55%
- **TV Everywhere usage**: ~48% of pay-TV subscribers

## 5. Language, Culture & Content Consumption

### Language
- **Primary language**: English, Irish (Gaeilge)
- **% bilingual population**: ~40% (Irish as second language)
- **Requirements**: English content, subtitling common

### Content Preferences
- **Top genres**: News, sports, movies, drama series, reality TV
- **Local content importance**: High - Irish content preferred
- **Sports rights**: Football (Premier League), Rugby, Gaelic Games, Horse Racing
- **Public service broadcaster influence**: Strong (RTÉ)

## 6. Regulatory Environment

### Broadcast Regulation
- **Licensing authorities**: BAI (Broadcasting Authority of Ireland)
- **Spectrum allocation**: DTT (Saorview), satellite (Astra, Eutelsat)
- **Must-carry rules**: Yes - public channels must be carried
- **Local content quota**: European content quotas apply

### Consumer & Platform Regulation
- **Anti-piracy environment**: Strong enforcement
- **CAS/DRM compliance**: Mandatory for pay-TV
- **CI+ certification requirements**: Yes, via CI+ Consortium
- **Taxes on TV subscriptions**: VAT 23%, TV Licence fee
- **Data protection**: GDPR compliant

## 7. Pay-TV, OTT, and Advertising Market KPIs

### Pay-TV
- **Pay-TV ARPU**: ~€50-60/month
- **Pay-TV revenue growth rate**: ~2-3% annually
- **Number of major operators**: 3-4 (Sky Ireland, Virgin Media Ireland, Eir, Vodafone Ireland)
- **Churn rate**: ~12-15% annually

### OTT/SVOD
- **Market size**: ~€400 million
- **Revenue/ARPU**: ~€10-12/month per service
- **Top players**: Netflix, Amazon Prime, Disney+, RTÉ Player, TG4 Player
- **Subscriber growth rates**: ~8-10% annually

### Advertising
- **TV advertising revenue**: ~€350 million
- **AVOD/CTV ad spend**: ~€90 million
- **Growth forecasts**: ~3-4% annually
- **Programmatic adoption**: ~45%

## 8. Market Forecasts

- **Pay-TV household forecast (5-10 years)**: Stable to slight decline (~-1% annually)
- **OTT/SVOD penetration forecast**: Growth to ~80% by 2030
- **Broadband and FTTH growth**: FTTH to reach ~80% by 2030
- **Economic forecast**: Strong growth (~3.0% GDP annually)
- **Population forecast**: Growth to ~5.5 million by 2030

## 9. Competitive Landscape

### Major Operators

**Sky Ireland**
- Platform type: Satellite, OTT
- Subscriber numbers: Largest pay-TV operator
- Market share: ~35% pay-TV
- Technology: NDS/Videoguard CAS, Sky Q hybrid STBs
- Features: 4K, Sky Q, catch-up TV, Sky Go

**Virgin Media Ireland**
- Platform type: Cable, OTT
- Subscriber numbers: Growing
- Market share: ~25% pay-TV
- Technology: CI+ CAM modules
- Features: 4K, Gigabit broadband, triple-play bundles

**Eir**
- Platform type: IPTV, OTT
- Subscriber numbers: Growing
- Market share: ~20% pay-TV
- Technology: Operator STBs, Eir TV platform
- Features: 4K, multi-device streaming

**Vodafone Ireland**
- Platform type: IPTV
- Subscriber numbers: Growing
- Market share: ~10% pay-TV
- Technology: Operator STBs
- Features: 4K, Vodafone TV
