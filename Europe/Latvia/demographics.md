# Latvia - Demographics & Market Data

## 1. Core Population & Household Structure

### Population Basics
- **Total population**: ~1.86 million (Jan 2025), declined by 18,400 (-1%) from previous year (largest annual decrease in nearly a decade)
- **Urban vs rural**: ~68% urban, ~32% rural
- **Population density**: ~29 people/km²
- **Household count**: ~800,000 households
- **Average household size**: ~2.3 people

### Age Structure
- **0-14**: ~15%
- **15-24**: ~11%
- **25-44**: ~26%
- **45-64**: ~28%
- **65+**: ~20%
- **Median age**: ~44 years

### Household Types
- **Single-person households**: ~36%
- **Family households with children**: ~32%
- **Multi-generational homes**: ~8%
- **Socioeconomic segmentation**: ABC1 ~40%, C2DE ~60%

## 2. Economic Indicators

### Macro-economics
- **GDP (total)**: ~€40 billion (2024)
- **GDP per capita**: ~€22,000
- **GDP growth rate**: ~2.0% (2024)
- **Inflation rate**: ~3.5% (2024)
- **Disposable income per household**: ~€19,000/year
- **Cost of living index**: 65 (EU average = 100)

### Consumer Spend Capacity
- **Average telecom spend per household**: ~€38/month
- **Average entertainment/media spend**: ~€48/month
- **Credit card penetration**: ~60%
- **Digital payments adoption**: ~70%

## 3. Connectivity & Device Penetration

### Broadband
- **Broadband penetration**: ~92.9% of population are online (early 2024)
- **Average broadband speed**: ~120 Mbps
- **FTTH availability**: ~50% of households use fiber connections (top European countries for fiber penetration), ~76% of fixed broadband subscriptions are over fiber (2022), ~38% deliver speeds above 300 Mbps, second-highest rural FTTP coverage in Europe
- **Mobile data penetration**: 4G/5G coverage ~95%
- **Mobile broadband ARPU**: ~€16/month

### Devices
- **Smart TV penetration**: ~60%
- **Streaming media players**: ~42%
- **Smartphone penetration**: ~88%
- **PC/tablet penetration**: ~80%
- **Console penetration**: ~32%

## 4. Television Access & Platform Penetration

### TV Platform Split
- **Total TV households**: ~800,000
- **Pay-TV households**: ~480,000 (~60%)
- **Free-to-air households**: ~320,000 (~40%)
- **Average daily TV viewing time**: ~260 minutes (highest in Baltics, 2024)

### Market share by delivery type:
- **Satellite**: ~25%
- **Cable**: ~30%
- **IPTV/FTTH**: ~35%
- **DTT**: ~10%
- **OTT-only households**: ~10%

### Platform Technology & CA
- **CI+ penetration**: ~40% of pay-TV households
- **CAM vs STB share**: ~30% CAM, ~70% STB
- **Conditional Access systems**: Conax, Irdeto, Nagravision
- **Smartcard vs cardless CAS**: Mixed (cardless growing)
- **Operator-owned STBs vs retail devices**: ~70% operator STBs

### OTT & Streaming
- **SVOD penetration**: ~50% of households
- **AVOD penetration**: ~45% of households
- **Hybrid STB penetration**: ~42%
- **TV Everywhere usage**: ~32% of pay-TV subscribers

## 5. Language, Culture & Content Consumption

### Language
- **Primary language**: Latvian
- **% bilingual population**: ~65% (English as second language)
- **Requirements**: Latvian subtitling preferred

### Content Preferences
- **Top genres**: News, sports, movies, drama series, documentaries
- **Local content importance**: High - Latvian content preferred
- **Sports rights**: Football, Basketball, Ice Hockey
- **Public service broadcaster influence**: Strong (LTV)

## 6. Regulatory Environment

### Broadcast Regulation
- **Licensing authorities**: NEPLP (National Electronic Mass Media Council)
- **Spectrum allocation**: DTT (MUX1-4), satellite (Eutelsat, SES)
- **Must-carry rules**: Yes - public channels must be carried
- **Local content quota**: European content quotas apply

### Consumer & Platform Regulation
- **Anti-piracy environment**: Moderate enforcement
- **CAS/DRM compliance**: Mandatory for pay-TV
- **CI+ certification requirements**: Yes, via CI+ Consortium
- **Taxes on TV subscriptions**: VAT 21%
- **Data protection**: GDPR compliant

## 7. Pay-TV, OTT, and Advertising Market KPIs

### Pay-TV
- **Pay-TV ARPU**: ~€22-30/month
- **Pay-TV revenue growth rate**: ~2-3% annually
- **Number of major operators**: 3-4 (Tet, Lattelecom, Baltcom, LMT)
- **Churn rate**: ~14-16% annually

### OTT/SVOD
- **Market size**: ~€70 million
- **Revenue/ARPU**: ~€7-9/month per service
- **Top players**: Netflix, HBO Max, Viaplay, Go3
- **Subscriber growth rates**: ~10-12% annually

### Advertising
- **TV advertising revenue**: ~€90 million
- **AVOD/CTV ad spend**: ~€20 million
- **Growth forecasts**: ~3-4% annually
- **Programmatic adoption**: ~35%

## 8. Market Forecasts

- **Pay-TV household forecast (5-10 years)**: Stable to slight decline (~-1% annually)
- **OTT/SVOD penetration forecast**: Growth to ~65% by 2030
- **Broadband and FTTH growth**: Fiber to reach ~85% by 2030
- **Economic forecast**: Moderate growth (~2.0% GDP annually)
- **Population forecast**: Continued decline (largest annual decrease in nearly a decade)
