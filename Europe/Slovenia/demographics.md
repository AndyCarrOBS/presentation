# Slovenia - Demographics & Market Data

## 1. Core Population & Household Structure

### Population Basics
- **Total population**: ~2.13 million (Jan 2025), +6,900 from previous year
- **Urban vs rural**: ~55% urban, ~45% rural
- **Population density**: ~105 people/km²
- **Household count**: ~837,000 households (2024)
- **Average household size**: ~2.4-2.7 people

### Age Structure
- **0-14**: ~14%
- **15-24**: ~11%
- **25-44**: ~26%
- **45-64**: ~28%
- **65+**: ~21%
- **Median age**: ~44 years

### Household Types
- **Single-person households**: ~32%
- **Family households with children**: ~38%
- **Multi-generational homes**: ~12%
- **Socioeconomic segmentation**: ABC1 ~40%, C2DE ~60%

## 2. Economic Indicators

### Macro-economics
- **GDP (total)**: ~€60 billion (2024)
- **GDP per capita**: ~€28,000
- **GDP growth rate**: ~2.0% (2024)
- **Inflation rate**: ~3.5% (2024)
- **Disposable income per household**: ~€24,000/year
- **Cost of living index**: 75 (EU average = 100)

### Consumer Spend Capacity
- **Average telecom spend per household**: ~€45/month
- **Average entertainment/media spend**: ~€55/month
- **Credit card penetration**: ~65%
- **Digital payments adoption**: ~70%

## 3. Connectivity & Device Penetration

### Broadband
- **Broadband penetration**: ~91.9% internet penetration (2024), ~1.95 million internet users
- **Average broadband speed**: ~90.47 kbit/s (2025 estimate)
- **FTTH availability**: ~50% (growing)
- **Mobile data penetration**: 4G/5G coverage ~95%, ~2.36 million active cellular mobile connections (111.3% of population, early 2024)
- **Mobile broadband ARPU**: ~€18/month

### Devices
- **Smart TV penetration**: ~58%
- **Streaming media players**: ~40%
- **Smartphone penetration**: ~88%
- **PC/tablet penetration**: ~82%
- **Console penetration**: ~35%

## 4. Television Access & Platform Penetration

### TV Platform Split
- **Total TV households**: ~837,000
- **Pay-TV households**: ~500,000 (~60%)
- **Free-to-air households**: ~337,000 (~40%)

### Market share by delivery type:
- **Satellite**: ~30%
- **Cable**: ~35%
- **IPTV/FTTH**: ~25%
- **DTT**: ~10%
- **OTT-only households**: ~10%

### Platform Technology & CA
- **CI+ penetration**: ~40% of pay-TV households
- **CAM vs STB share**: ~30% CAM, ~70% STB
- **Conditional Access systems**: Nagravision, Irdeto, Conax
- **Smartcard vs cardless CAS**: Smartcard dominant
- **Operator-owned STBs vs retail devices**: ~65% operator STBs

### OTT & Streaming
- **SVOD penetration**: ~45% of households
- **AVOD penetration**: ~40% of households
- **Hybrid STB penetration**: ~40%
- **TV Everywhere usage**: ~30% of pay-TV subscribers

## 5. Language, Culture & Content Consumption

### Language
- **Primary language**: Slovenian
- **% bilingual population**: ~60% (English as second language)
- **Requirements**: Slovenian dubbing/subtitling preferred

### Content Preferences
- **Top genres**: News, sports, movies, drama series, reality TV
- **Local content importance**: High - Slovenian content preferred
- **Sports rights**: Football, Basketball, Skiing
- **Public service broadcaster influence**: Strong (RTV Slovenija)

## 6. Regulatory Environment

### Broadcast Regulation
- **Licensing authorities**: AVP (Agency for Communication Networks and Services)
- **Spectrum allocation**: DTT (MUX1-4), satellite (Eutelsat, SES)
- **Must-carry rules**: Yes - public channels must be carried
- **Local content quota**: European content quotas apply

### Consumer & Platform Regulation
- **Anti-piracy environment**: Moderate enforcement
- **CAS/DRM compliance**: Mandatory for pay-TV
- **CI+ certification requirements**: Yes, via CI+ Consortium
- **Taxes on TV subscriptions**: VAT 22%
- **Data protection**: GDPR compliant

## 7. Pay-TV, OTT, and Advertising Market KPIs

### Pay-TV
- **Pay-TV ARPU**: ~€30-40/month
- **Pay-TV revenue growth rate**: ~2-3% annually
- **Number of major operators**: 4-5 (Telekom Slovenije, A1 Slovenia, Telemach, T-2)
- **Churn rate**: ~14-16% annually

### OTT/SVOD
- **Market size**: ~€150 million
- **Revenue/ARPU**: ~€8-10/month per service
- **Top players**: Netflix, HBO Max, Voyo, O2 TV Slovenia
- **Subscriber growth rates**: ~10-12% annually

### Advertising
- **TV advertising revenue**: ~€200 million
- **AVOD/CTV ad spend**: ~€40 million
- **Growth forecasts**: ~3-4% annually
- **Programmatic adoption**: ~35%

## 8. Market Forecasts

- **Pay-TV household forecast (5-10 years)**: Stable to slight growth (~1% annually)
- **OTT/SVOD penetration forecast**: Growth to ~60% by 2030
- **Broadband and FTTH growth**: FTTH to reach ~70% by 2030
- **Economic forecast**: Moderate growth (~2.0% GDP annually)
- **Population forecast**: Stable to slight decline (~2.1 million by 2030, aging population, fertility rate 1.6)
