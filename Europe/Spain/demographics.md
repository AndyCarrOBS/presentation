- **Population density**: ~96 people/km²
- **Household count**: ~19.35 million households (2024)
- **Average household size**: ~2.5 people

### Age Structure
- **0-14**: ~14%
- **15-24**: ~10%
- **25-44**: ~26%
- **45-64**: ~27%
- **65+**: ~23%
- **Median age**: ~45 years

### Household Types

- **Family households with children**: ~45%
- **Multi-generational homes**: ~12%

## 5. Language, Culture & Content Consumption

### Language
- **Primary language**: Spanish (Castilian)
- **% bilingual population**: ~25% (Catalan, Basque, Galician)
- **Requirements**: Spanish dubbing preferred, subtitling common

### Content Preferences
- **Top genres**: News, sports, movies, drama series, reality TV, telenovelas
- **Local content importance**: Very high - Spanish content preferred
- **Sports rights**: Football (La Liga), Basketball, Tennis
- **Public service broadcaster influence**: Strong (RTVE)

## 6. Regulatory Environment

### Broadcast Regulation
- **Licensing authorities**: CNMC (Comisión Nacional de los Mercados y la Competencia)
- **Spectrum allocation**: DTT (MUX1-8), satellite (Hispasat, Eutelsat)
- **Must-carry rules**: Yes - public channels must be carried
- **Local content quota**: European content quotas apply

### Consumer & Platform Regulation
- **Anti-piracy environment**: Moderate enforcement
- **CAS/DRM compliance**: Mandatory for pay-TV
- **CI+ certification requirements**: Yes, via CI+ Consortium
- **Taxes on TV subscriptions**: VAT 21%
- **Data protection**: GDPR compliant
