# North Macedonia - Demographics & Market Data

## 1. Core Population & Household Structure

### Population Basics
- **Total population**: ~2.0 million (2024 World Population Data Sheet)
- **Urban vs rural**: ~58% urban, ~42% rural
- **Population density**: ~78 people/km²
- **Household count**: ~600,000 households
- **Average household size**: ~3.3 people

### Age Structure
- **0-14**: ~16%
- **15-24**: ~13%
- **25-44**: ~28%
- **45-64**: ~26%
- **65+**: ~17%
- **Median age**: ~40 years

### Household Types
- **Single-person households**: ~18%
- **Family households with children**: ~52%
- **Multi-generational homes**: ~20%
- **Socioeconomic segmentation**: ABC1 ~28%, C2DE ~72%

## 2. Economic Indicators

### Macro-economics
- **GDP (total)**: ~€13 billion (2024)
- **GDP per capita**: ~€6,500
- **GDP growth rate**: ~2.5% (2024)
- **Inflation rate**: ~4.5% (2024)
- **Disposable income per household**: ~€8,000/year
- **Cost of living index**: 35 (EU average = 100)

### Consumer Spend Capacity
- **Average telecom spend per household**: ~€24/month
- **Average entertainment/media spend**: ~€30/month
- **Credit card penetration**: ~32%
- **Digital payments adoption**: ~42%

## 3. Connectivity & Device Penetration

### Broadband
- **Broadband penetration**: ~53.8% of population use internet (2021)
- **Average broadband speed**: ~75 Mbps
- **FTTH availability**: ~30% (growing)
- **Mobile data penetration**: 4G/5G coverage ~90%
- **Mobile broadband ARPU**: ~€10/month

### Devices
- **Smart TV penetration**: ~42%
- **Streaming media players**: ~20%
- **Smartphone penetration**: ~72%
- **PC/tablet penetration**: ~62%
- **Console penetration**: ~16%

## 4. Television Access & Platform Penetration

### TV Platform Split
- **Total TV households**: ~600,000
- **Pay-TV households**: ~360,000 (~60%)
- **Free-to-air households**: ~240,000 (~40%)

### Market share by delivery type:
- **Satellite**: ~35%
- **Cable**: ~30%
- **IPTV/FTTH**: ~25%
- **DTT**: ~10%
- **OTT-only households**: ~8%

### Platform Technology & CA
- **CI+ penetration**: ~30% of pay-TV households
- **CAM vs STB share**: ~25% CAM, ~75% STB
- **Conditional Access systems**: Nagravision, Irdeto, Conax
- **Smartcard vs cardless CAS**: Smartcard dominant
- **Operator-owned STBs vs retail devices**: ~70% operator STBs

### OTT & Streaming
- **SVOD penetration**: ~30% of households
- **AVOD penetration**: ~26% of households
- **Hybrid STB penetration**: ~24%
- **TV Everywhere usage**: ~18% of pay-TV subscribers

## 5. Language, Culture & Content Consumption

### Language
- **Primary language**: Macedonian, Albanian
- **% bilingual population**: ~40% (multilingual country)
- **Requirements**: Content in Macedonian/Albanian depending on region

### Content Preferences
- **Top genres**: News, sports, movies, drama series, reality TV
- **Local content importance**: Very high - Macedonian content preferred
- **Sports rights**: Football, Basketball, Handball
- **Public service broadcaster influence**: Strong (MRT)

## 6. Regulatory Environment

### Broadcast Regulation
- **Licensing authorities**: AVMU (Agency for Audio and Audiovisual Media Services)
- **Spectrum allocation**: DTT, satellite (Eutelsat, SES)
- **Must-carry rules**: Yes - public channels must be carried
- **Local content quota**: European content quotas apply

### Consumer & Platform Regulation
- **Anti-piracy environment**: Moderate enforcement
- **CAS/DRM compliance**: Mandatory for pay-TV
- **CI+ certification requirements**: Yes, via CI+ Consortium
- **Taxes on TV subscriptions**: VAT 18%
- **Data protection**: GDPR compliant

## 7. Pay-TV, OTT, and Advertising Market KPIs

### Pay-TV
- **Pay-TV ARPU**: ~€15-23/month
- **Pay-TV revenue growth rate**: ~4-5% annually
- **Number of major operators**: 3-4 (Telekom North Macedonia, A1 North Macedonia, Telemach)
- **Churn rate**: ~18-20% annually

### OTT/SVOD
- **Market size**: ~€50 million
- **Revenue/ARPU**: ~€6-8/month per service
- **Top players**: Netflix, HBO Max, Telemach TV Online
- **Subscriber growth rates**: ~15-18% annually

### Advertising
- **TV advertising revenue**: ~€60 million
- **AVOD/CTV ad spend**: ~€12 million
- **Growth forecasts**: ~5-6% annually
- **Programmatic adoption**: ~24%

## 8. Market Forecasts

- **Pay-TV household forecast (5-10 years)**: Growth (~2% annually)
- **OTT/SVOD penetration forecast**: Growth to ~48% by 2030
- **Broadband and FTTH growth**: FTTH to reach ~50% by 2030
- **Economic forecast**: Moderate growth (~2.5% GDP annually)
- **Population forecast**: Stable to slight decline (~1.9 million by 2030)
