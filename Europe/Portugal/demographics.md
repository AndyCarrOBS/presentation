# Portugal - Demographics & Market Data

## 1. Core Population & Household Structure

### Population Basics
- **Total population**: ~10.24 million (2024)
- **Urban vs rural**: ~68.1% urban, ~31.9% rural
- **Population density**: ~111 people/km²
- **Household count**: ~4.1 million households
- **Average household size**: ~2.5 people

### Age Structure
- **0-14**: ~13%
- **15-24**: ~11%
- **25-44**: ~25%
- **45-64**: ~28%
- **65+**: ~23%
- **Median age**: ~46 years

### Household Types
- **Single-person households**: ~30%
- **Family households with children**: ~40%
- **Multi-generational homes**: ~15%
- **Socioeconomic segmentation**: ABC1 ~40%, C2DE ~60%

## 2. Economic Indicators

### Macro-economics
- **GDP (total)**: ~€250 billion (2024)
- **GDP per capita**: ~€24,000
- **GDP growth rate**: ~2.2% (2024)
- **Inflation rate**: ~3.0% (2024)
- **Disposable income per household**: ~€22,000/year
- **Cost of living index**: 80 (EU average = 100)

### Consumer Spend Capacity
- **Average telecom spend per household**: ~€50/month
- **Average entertainment/media spend**: ~€60/month
- **Credit card penetration**: ~70%
- **Digital payments adoption**: ~65%

## 3. Connectivity & Device Penetration

### Broadband
- **Broadband penetration**: ~95% of households
- **Average broadband speed**: ~150 Mbps
- **FTTH availability**: ~92.3% FTTP coverage (68.7% in rural areas)
- **Mobile data penetration**: 4G/5G coverage ~98%
- **Mobile broadband ARPU**: ~€22/month
- **Cable modem services**: ~57.8% of households (35.3% in rural areas)

### Devices
- **Smart TV penetration**: ~70%
- **Streaming media players**: ~45%
- **Smartphone penetration**: ~88%
- **PC/tablet penetration**: ~80%
- **Console penetration**: ~38%

## 4. Television Access & Platform Penetration

### TV Platform Split
- **Total TV households**: ~4.1 million
- **Pay-TV households**: ~3.6 million (~88.7% subscribe to pay-TV services)
- **Free-to-air households**: ~500,000 (~11.3%)

### Market share by delivery type:
- **Satellite**: ~30%
- **Cable**: ~40%
- **IPTV/FTTH**: ~25%
- **DTT**: ~23.7% of households access DTT (only 7.4% exclusively DTT, declining -9.4pp from 2023)
- **OTT-only households**: ~15%

### Platform Technology & CA
- **CI+ penetration**: ~50% of pay-TV households
- **CAM vs STB share**: ~35% CAM, ~65% STB
- **Conditional Access systems**: Nagravision, Irdeto, Viaccess
- **Smartcard vs cardless CAS**: Mixed (smartcard dominant)
- **Operator-owned STBs vs retail devices**: ~70% operator STBs

### OTT & Streaming
- **SVOD penetration**: ~55% of households
- **AVOD penetration**: ~48% of households
- **Hybrid STB penetration**: ~50%
- **TV Everywhere usage**: ~40% of pay-TV subscribers

## 5. Language, Culture & Content Consumption

### Language
- **Primary language**: Portuguese
- **% bilingual population**: ~35% (English as second language)
- **Requirements**: Portuguese dubbing/subtitling preferred

### Content Preferences
- **Top genres**: News, sports, movies, drama series, reality TV, telenovelas
- **Local content importance**: Very high - Portuguese content preferred
- **Sports rights**: Football (Primeira Liga), Futsal
- **Public service broadcaster influence**: Strong (RTP)

## 6. Regulatory Environment

### Broadcast Regulation
- **Licensing authorities**: ERC (Entidade Reguladora para a Comunicação Social)
- **Spectrum allocation**: DTT (MUX1-4), satellite (Hispasat, Eutelsat)
- **Must-carry rules**: Yes - public channels must be carried
- **Local content quota**: European content quotas apply

### Consumer & Platform Regulation
- **Anti-piracy environment**: Moderate enforcement
- **CAS/DRM compliance**: Mandatory for pay-TV
- **CI+ certification requirements**: Yes, via CI+ Consortium
- **Taxes on TV subscriptions**: VAT 23%
- **Data protection**: GDPR compliant

## 7. Pay-TV, OTT, and Advertising Market KPIs

### Pay-TV
- **Pay-TV ARPU**: ~€35-45/month
- **Pay-TV revenue growth rate**: ~2-3% annually
- **Number of major operators**: 4-5 (NOS, MEO, Vodafone Portugal, Nowo)
- **Churn rate**: ~12-15% annually

### OTT/SVOD
- **Market size**: ~€400 million
- **Revenue/ARPU**: ~€9-11/month per service
- **Top players**: Netflix, HBO Portugal, Disney+, Amazon Prime, RTP Play
- **Subscriber growth rates**: ~8-10% annually

### Advertising
- **TV advertising revenue**: ~€600 million
- **AVOD/CTV ad spend**: ~€120 million
- **Growth forecasts**: ~2-3% annually
- **Programmatic adoption**: ~40%

## 8. Market Forecasts

- **Pay-TV household forecast (5-10 years)**: Stable to slight decline (~-1% annually)
- **OTT/SVOD penetration forecast**: Growth to ~70% by 2030
- **Broadband and FTTH growth**: FTTP to reach ~98% by 2030
- **Economic forecast**: Moderate growth (~2.2% GDP annually)
- **Population forecast**: Decline to ~9.8 million by 2030

## 9. Competitive Landscape

### Major Operators

**NOS**
- Platform type: Cable, IPTV, Satellite
- Subscriber numbers: Largest pay-TV operator
- Market share: ~35% pay-TV
- Technology: CI+ CAM modules, operator STBs
- Features: 4K, NOS Play OTT, triple-play bundles

**MEO (Altice Portugal)**
- Platform type: IPTV, Cable
- Subscriber numbers: Growing
- Market share: ~30% pay-TV
- Technology: Operator STBs, MEO Go OTT
- Features: 4K, multi-device streaming

**Vodafone Portugal**
- Platform type: IPTV
- Subscriber numbers: Growing
- Market share: ~20% pay-TV
- Technology: Operator STBs
- Features: 4K, Vodafone TV

**Nowo**
- Platform type: Cable, IPTV
- Subscriber numbers: Growing
- Market share: ~8% pay-TV
- Technology: CI+ CAM modules
- Features: Triple-play bundles
