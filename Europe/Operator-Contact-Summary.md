## Recommended Contact Approach

### For Standard Specifications (CI+, HbbTV)

1. Access publicly available specifications from:
   - CI+ Consortium: https://www.ci-plus.com/
   - HbbTV Consortium: https://www.hbbtv.org/

### For Operator-Specific Specifications

1. **Check Operator Website**: Look for "Partner", "Developer", or "Business" sections
2. **Contact Business Development**: Primary contact point for partnership discussions
3. **Developer Portal**: Register/access if available
4. **Parent Company Programs**: For operators under large groups (Liberty Global, Deutsche Telekom, etc.), check parent company partner programs
5. **M7 Group Operators**: Contact M7 Group directly (info@m7group.eu) for unified platform specifications

### For CAS Vendor Specifications

1. **Nagravision**: Contact Nagravision directly (may require NDA)
2. **Irdeto**: Contact Irdeto directly (may require NDA)
3. **Conax**: Contact Conax directly (may require NDA)
4. **Viaccess**: Contact Viaccess directly (may require NDA)

## Access Process

1. **Initial Contact**: Visit operator website and locate business development or partner program contact
2. **Partnership Inquiry**: Contact business development department via website contact form or email
3. **Technical Request**: Request access to technical specifications and developer documentation
4. **Agreement**: May require partnership agreement or NDA for detailed specifications
5. **M7 Group**: For M7 Group operators, contact M7 Group directly for unified platform specifications

## Notes

- All contact information is stored in individual operator specification files
- Contact information may require verification through operator websites
- Some operators may require partnership agreements before providing technical specifications
- Parent company partner programs often provide unified access to multiple operators
- M7 Group operators share unified technical specifications across platforms
