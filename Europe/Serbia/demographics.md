# Serbia - Demographics & Market Data

## 1. Core Population & Household Structure

### Population Basics
- **Total population**: ~8.62 million (2024)
- **Urban vs rural**: ~56% urban, ~44% rural
- **Population density**: ~98 people/km²
- **Household count**: ~2.54 million households
- **Average household size**: ~3.4 people

### Age Structure
- **0-14**: ~15%
- **15-24**: ~12%
- **25-44**: ~27%
- **45-64**: ~27%
- **65+**: ~19%
- **Median age**: ~43 years

### Household Types
- **Single-person households**: ~20%
- **Family households with children**: ~50%
- **Multi-generational homes**: ~20%
- **Socioeconomic segmentation**: ABC1 ~30%, C2DE ~70%

## 2. Economic Indicators

### Macro-economics
- **GDP (total)**: ~€60 billion (2024)
- **GDP per capita**: ~€7,000
- **GDP growth rate**: ~3.0% (2024)
- **Inflation rate**: ~5.0% (2024)
- **Disposable income per household**: ~€10,000/year
- **Cost of living index**: 40 (EU average = 100)

### Consumer Spend Capacity
- **Average telecom spend per household**: ~€28/month
- **Average entertainment/media spend**: ~€35/month
- **Credit card penetration**: ~40%
- **Digital payments adoption**: ~50%

## 3. Connectivity & Device Penetration

### Broadband
- **Broadband penetration**: ~83.5% of households have internet access, ~29.3% fixed broadband penetration per 100 inhabitants
- **Average broadband speed**: ~90 Mbps
- **FTTH availability**: ~35% (growing)
- **Mobile data penetration**: 4G/5G coverage ~95%, ~109.5% mobile broadband penetration per 100 inhabitants
- **Mobile broadband ARPU**: ~€12/month

### Devices
- **Smart TV penetration**: ~48%
- **Streaming media players**: ~28%
- **Smartphone penetration**: ~78%
- **PC/tablet penetration**: ~68%
- **Console penetration**: ~22%

## 4. Television Access & Platform Penetration

### TV Platform Split
- **Total TV households**: ~2.54 million
- **Pay-TV households**: ~1.5 million (~60%)
- **Free-to-air households**: ~1.04 million (~40%)

### Market share by delivery type:
- **Satellite**: ~35%
- **Cable**: ~30%
- **IPTV/FTTH**: ~25%
- **DTT**: ~10%
- **OTT-only households**: ~8%

### Platform Technology & CA
- **CI+ penetration**: ~35% of pay-TV households
- **CAM vs STB share**: ~25% CAM, ~75% STB
- **Conditional Access systems**: Nagravision, Irdeto, Conax
- **Smartcard vs cardless CAS**: Smartcard dominant
- **Operator-owned STBs vs retail devices**: ~70% operator STBs

### OTT & Streaming
- **SVOD penetration**: ~32% of households
- **AVOD penetration**: ~28% of households
- **Hybrid STB penetration**: ~28%
- **TV Everywhere usage**: ~20% of pay-TV subscribers

## 5. Language, Culture & Content Consumption

### Language
- **Primary language**: Serbian
- **% bilingual population**: ~35% (English as second language)
- **Requirements**: Serbian dubbing/subtitling preferred

### Content Preferences
- **Top genres**: News, sports, movies, drama series, reality TV
- **Local content importance**: Very high - Serbian content preferred
- **Sports rights**: Football, Basketball, Tennis
- **Public service broadcaster influence**: Strong (RTS)

## 6. Regulatory Environment

### Broadcast Regulation
- **Licensing authorities**: REM (Regulatory Authority for Electronic Media)
- **Spectrum allocation**: DTT, satellite (Eutelsat, SES)
- **Must-carry rules**: Yes - public channels must be carried
- **Local content quota**: European content quotas apply

### Consumer & Platform Regulation
- **Anti-piracy environment**: Moderate enforcement
- **CAS/DRM compliance**: Mandatory for pay-TV
- **CI+ certification requirements**: Yes, via CI+ Consortium
- **Taxes on TV subscriptions**: VAT 20%
- **Data protection**: GDPR compliant

## 7. Pay-TV, OTT, and Advertising Market KPIs

### Pay-TV
- **Pay-TV ARPU**: ~€18-28/month
- **Pay-TV revenue growth rate**: ~4-5% annually
- **Number of major operators**: 4-5 (Telekom Srbija, SBB, Supernova, Total TV)
- **Churn rate**: ~18-20% annually

### OTT/SVOD
- **Market size**: ~€120 million
- **Revenue/ARPU**: ~€7-9/month per service
- **Top players**: Netflix, HBO Max, Supernova TV, Total TV Online
- **Subscriber growth rates**: ~15-18% annually

### Advertising
- **TV advertising revenue**: ~€150 million
- **AVOD/CTV ad spend**: ~€30 million
- **Growth forecasts**: ~5-6% annually
- **Programmatic adoption**: ~28%

## 8. Market Forecasts

- **Pay-TV household forecast (5-10 years)**: Growth (~2% annually)
- **OTT/SVOD penetration forecast**: Growth to ~50% by 2030
- **Broadband and FTTH growth**: FTTH to reach ~55% by 2030
- **Economic forecast**: Moderate growth (~3.0% GDP annually)
- **Population forecast**: Decline to ~8.3 million by 2030

## 9. Competitive Landscape

### Major Operators

**Telekom Srbija**
- Platform type: IPTV, Cable
- Subscriber numbers: Largest pay-TV operator
- Market share: ~35% pay-TV
- Technology: Operator STBs, Telekom TV platform
- Features: 4K, multi-device streaming

**SBB (Serbian Broadband)**
- Platform type: Cable
- Subscriber numbers: Growing
- Market share: ~25% pay-TV
- Technology: CI+ CAM modules
- Features: Triple-play bundles

**Supernova**
- Platform type: Cable, IPTV
- Subscriber numbers: Growing
- Market share: ~20% pay-TV
- Technology: CI+ CAM modules
- Features: Supernova TV Online

**Total TV**
- Platform type: Satellite, IPTV
- Subscriber numbers: Growing
- Market share: ~12% pay-TV
- Technology: Smartcards, CI+ compatibility varies
- Features: Total TV Online
