# TV Platform Operator: Deutsche Telekom Magenta TV

## 1. Executive Summary

- **Overview of the operator's business**: Deutsche Telekom Magenta TV is the IPTV and streaming service of Deutsche Telekom, Germany's largest telecommunications company. The service offers TV content via IPTV (over broadband) and satellite, integrated with Deutsche Telekom's broadband and mobile services.

- **Key market position**: #2 IPTV provider in Germany, ~12% pay-TV market share, ~4.725 million TV subscribers (as of September 2025), part of Germany's largest telco

- **Geographic footprint**: Nationwide coverage in Germany through Deutsche Telekom's extensive broadband network

- **Ownership structure**: Part of Deutsche Telekom AG (German telecommunications company, partially state-owned)

- **Overall growth/decline assessment**: Growing - steady subscriber growth (2.9% YoY increase as of September 2025), strategic partnerships with content providers, integration with RTL+ streaming service

## 4. Growth Assessment

### 4.1 Market Position

- Ranking vs. competitors: #2 IPTV provider, #1 telecommunications provider in Germany
- Strength in broadband, mobile, and TV: Strong in all three segments, largest mobile and broadband provider, growing TV presence
- Differentiators: Integrated quad-play offerings, RTL+ Premium included in most Magenta TV plans, extensive broadband network, FTTH expansion

### 4.2 Growth Indicators

- Subscriber trends across FTTH, broadband, TV, and mobile: TV subscriber growth (+2.9% YoY), mobile customer base stable, broadband network expansion
- ARPU trends: Data not publicly disclosed
- Network upgrades: Significant investment in FTTH expansion, gigabit network upgrades
- New service launches: RTL+ Premium integration (renewed until at least 2030), enhanced streaming capabilities, 4K/UHD content

### 4.3 Decline Indicators

- Cord-cutting pressure: Moderate pressure from pure OTT competitors
- Regulatory or pricing pressure: Standard regulatory environment
- Content rights losses: Standard content portfolio maintained, strengthened with RTL+ partnership
- Competitive erosion from OTT: Competition from Netflix, Disney+, Amazon Prime Video, but also offers integrated OTT services and RTL+ Premium included

### **Overall Assessment**

- **Operator Status:** Growth

- **Drivers Behind the Trend:** Steady subscriber growth (+2.9% YoY) demonstrates strong market position. Strategic partnership with RTL Deutschland (RTL+ Premium included in most Magenta TV plans until at least 2030) enhances content offering and provides competitive advantage. Integration with Deutsche Telekom's extensive broadband and mobile network enables converged service offerings. FTTH expansion provides future growth opportunities.
