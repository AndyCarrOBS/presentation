# Malta - Demographics & Market Data

## 1. Core Population & Household Structure

### Population Basics
- **Total population**: ~516,000 (2024)
- **Urban vs rural**: ~95% urban, ~5% rural
- **Population density**: ~1,633 people/km²
- **Household count**: ~150,000 households
- **Average household size**: ~3.4 people

### Age Structure
- **0-14**: ~18.1% (0-4: 4.9%, 5-12: 8.2%, 13-17: 5.0%)
- **15-24**: ~7.6% (18-24)
- **25-44**: ~32.7% (25-34: 15.2%, 35-44: 17.5%)
- **45-64**: ~26.0% (45-54: 14.0%, 55-64: 12.0%)
- **65+**: ~15.6%
- **Median age**: ~42 years

### Household Types
- **Single-person households**: ~20%
- **Family households with children**: ~50%
- **Multi-generational homes**: ~20%
- **Socioeconomic segmentation**: ABC1 ~48%, C2DE ~52%

## 5. Language, Culture & Content Consumption

### Language
- **Primary language**: Maltese, English (both official)
- **% bilingual population**: ~90% (English as second language)
- **Requirements**: Content in Maltese/English

### Content Preferences
- **Top genres**: News, sports, movies, drama series, reality TV
- **Local content importance**: High - Maltese content preferred
- **Sports rights**: Football, Rugby
- **Public service broadcaster influence**: Strong (PBS)

## 6. Regulatory Environment

### Broadcast Regulation
- **Licensing authorities**: BA (Broadcasting Authority)
- **Spectrum allocation**: DTT, satellite (Eutelsat, SES)
- **Must-carry rules**: Yes - public channels must be carried
- **Local content quota**: European content quotas apply

### Consumer & Platform Regulation
- **Anti-piracy environment**: Moderate enforcement
- **CAS/DRM compliance**: Mandatory for pay-TV
- **CI+ certification requirements**: Yes, via CI+ Consortium
- **Taxes on TV subscriptions**: VAT 18%
- **Data protection**: GDPR compliant
