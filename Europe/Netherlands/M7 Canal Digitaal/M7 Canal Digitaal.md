# TV Platform Operator: M7 Canal Digitaal (CANAL+ Netherlands)

## 1. Executive Summary

- **Overview of the operator's business**: M7 Canal Digitaal, rebranded as CANAL+ Netherlands in January 2024, is a satellite pay-TV platform in the Netherlands, operated by M7 Group. The platform provides premium content including sports, movies, series, and entertainment channels via satellite delivery, now combined with streaming services.

- **Key market position**: Satellite pay-TV platform in the Netherlands, part of M7 Group with 3.6 million subscribers across European operations (2023)

- **Geographic footprint**: Nationwide coverage in the Netherlands via satellite

- **Ownership structure**: Part of M7 Group (owned by Canal+ Group/Vivendi)

- **Overall growth/decline assessment**: Transition - Rebranded to CANAL+ Netherlands (January 2024) combining live TV and streaming services. M7 Group reported 3.6 million subscribers across European operations (2023). Strategic shift to enhance offerings with streaming platform integration.
