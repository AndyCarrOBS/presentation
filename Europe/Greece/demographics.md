# Greece - Demographics & Market Data

## 1. Core Population & Household Structure

### Population Basics
- **Total population**: ~10.49 million (2024), growth rate -0.47%
- **Urban vs rural**: ~80% urban, ~20% rural
- **Population density**: ~79 people/km²
- **Household count**: ~4.2 million households
- **Average household size**: ~2.5 people

### Age Structure
- **0-14**: ~13.9%
- **15-24**: ~10%
- **25-44**: ~26%
- **45-64**: ~28%
- **65+**: ~23.6%
- **Median age**: ~46 years

### Household Types
- **Single-person households**: ~32%
- **Family households with children**: ~38%
- **Multi-generational homes**: ~15%
- **Socioeconomic segmentation**: ABC1 ~38%, C2DE ~62%

## 2. Economic Indicators

### Macro-economics
- **GDP (total)**: ~€220 billion (2024)
- **GDP per capita**: ~€21,000
- **GDP growth rate**: ~2.0% (2024)
- **Inflation rate**: ~3.5% (2024)
- **Disposable income per household**: ~€19,000/year
- **Cost of living index**: 75 (EU average = 100)

### Consumer Spend Capacity
- **Average telecom spend per household**: ~€45/month
- **Average entertainment/media spend**: ~€55/month
- **Credit card penetration**: ~65%
- **Digital payments adoption**: ~60%

## 3. Connectivity & Device Penetration

### Broadband
- **Broadband penetration**: ~86.2% internet penetration (2024), ~43.2% fixed broadband penetration
- **Average broadband speed**: ~120 Mbps
- **FTTH availability**: ~35% (growing)
- **Mobile data penetration**: 4G/5G coverage ~95%
- **Mobile broadband ARPU**: ~€20/month
- **Ultra-high-speed broadband (≥100 Mbps)**: ~37.4% of broadband lines

### Devices
- **Smart TV penetration**: ~58%
- **Streaming media players**: ~40%
- **Smartphone penetration**: ~85%
- **PC/tablet penetration**: ~75%
- **Console penetration**: ~35%

## 4. Television Access & Platform Penetration

### TV Platform Split
- **Total TV households**: ~4.2 million
- **Pay-TV households**: ~725,594 TV subscribers (OTE, Dec 2024), +7.2% YoY
- **Free-to-air households**: ~3.5 million (~83%)

### Market share by delivery type:
- **Satellite**: ~30%
- **Cable**: ~20%
- **IPTV/FTTH**: ~40%
- **DTT**: ~10%
- **OTT-only households**: ~12%

### Platform Technology & CA
- **CI+ penetration**: ~35% of pay-TV households
- **CAM vs STB share**: ~25% CAM, ~75% STB
- **Conditional Access systems**: Nagravision, Irdeto, Viaccess
- **Smartcard vs cardless CAS**: Smartcard dominant
- **Operator-owned STBs vs retail devices**: ~70% operator STBs

### OTT & Streaming
- **SVOD penetration**: ~45% of households
- **AVOD penetration**: ~40% of households
- **Hybrid STB penetration**: ~40%
- **TV Everywhere usage**: ~30% of pay-TV subscribers

## 5. Language, Culture & Content Consumption

### Language
- **Primary language**: Greek
- **% bilingual population**: ~30% (English as second language)
- **Requirements**: Greek dubbing/subtitling preferred

### Content Preferences
- **Top genres**: News, sports, movies, drama series, reality TV
- **Local content importance**: Very high - Greek content preferred
- **Sports rights**: Football (Super League), Basketball, Volleyball
- **Public service broadcaster influence**: Strong (ERT)

## 6. Regulatory Environment

### Broadcast Regulation
- **Licensing authorities**: NCRTV (National Council for Radio and Television)
- **Spectrum allocation**: DTT (MUX1-4), satellite (Eutelsat, SES)
- **Must-carry rules**: Yes - public channels must be carried
- **Local content quota**: European content quotas apply

### Consumer & Platform Regulation
- **Anti-piracy environment**: Moderate enforcement
- **CAS/DRM compliance**: Mandatory for pay-TV
- **CI+ certification requirements**: Yes, via CI+ Consortium
- **Taxes on TV subscriptions**: VAT 24%
- **Data protection**: GDPR compliant

## 7. Pay-TV, OTT, and Advertising Market KPIs

### Pay-TV
- **Pay-TV ARPU**: ~€30-40/month
- **Pay-TV revenue growth rate**: ~2-3% annually
- **Number of major operators**: 4-5 (Cosmote TV/OTE, Nova, Forthnet, Vodafone Greece, Cyta Hellas)
- **Churn rate**: ~14-16% annually

### OTT/SVOD
- **Market size**: ~€300 million
- **Revenue/ARPU**: ~€8-10/month per service
- **Top players**: Netflix, Cosmote TV, Nova Play, Disney+, Amazon Prime
- **Subscriber growth rates**: ~10-12% annually

### Advertising
- **TV advertising revenue**: ~€500 million
- **AVOD/CTV ad spend**: ~€100 million
- **Growth forecasts**: ~2-3% annually
- **Programmatic adoption**: ~38%

## 8. Market Forecasts

- **Pay-TV household forecast (5-10 years)**: Stable to slight growth (~1% annually)
- **OTT/SVOD penetration forecast**: Growth to ~60% by 2030
- **Broadband and FTTH growth**: FTTH to reach ~55% by 2030
- **Economic forecast**: Moderate growth (~2.0% GDP annually)
- **Population forecast**: Decline to ~10.2 million by 2030
