# United Kingdom - Demographics & Market Data

- **Total population**: ~68.3 million (mid-2023), projected ~72.5 million by 2032
- **Urban vs rural**: ~84% urban, ~16% rural
- **Population density**: ~281 people/km²
- **Household count**: ~28.6 million households (2024)
- **Average household size**: ~2.35 people

### Age Structure
- **0-14**: ~17%
- **15-24**: ~12%
- **25-44**: ~26%
- **45-64**: ~26%
- **65+**: ~19%
- **Median age**: ~41 years

### Household Types
- **Single-person households**: ~29.5%
- **Family households with children**: ~66.9% contain one family
- **Multi-generational homes**: ~5%
- **Socioeconomic segmentation**: ABC1 ~55%, C2DE ~45%

- **GDP (total)**: ~£2.3 trillion (2024)

## 4. Television Access & Platform Penetration

### TV Platform Split
- **Total TV households**: ~27.52 million (Q4 2024)
- **Pay-TV households**: ~18 million (~65%)
- **Free-to-air households**: ~9.5 million (~35%)

### Market share by delivery type:
- **Satellite**: ~28% (Sky)
- **Cable**: ~12% (Virgin Media)
- **IPTV/FTTH**: ~25%
- **DTT**: ~17% (Freeview)

### Platform Technology & CA
- **CI+ penetration**: ~30% of pay-TV households
- **CAM vs STB share**: ~25% CAM, ~75% STB
- **Conditional Access systems**: NDS/Videoguard (Sky), Nagravision
- **Smartcard vs cardless CAS**: Mixed (cardless growing)
- **Operator-owned STBs vs retail devices**: ~80% operator STBs

### OTT & Streaming
- **SVOD penetration**: ~75% of households
- **AVOD penetration**: ~60% of households
- **Hybrid STB penetration**: ~65%
- **TV Everywhere usage**: ~50% of pay-TV subscribers

## 5. Language, Culture & Content Consumption

### Language
- **Primary language**: English
- **% bilingual population**: ~20% (various languages)
- **Requirements**: English content, subtitling common

### Content Preferences
- **Top genres**: News, sports, drama series, reality TV, documentaries
- **Local content importance**: High - UK content preferred
- **Sports rights**: Football (Premier League), Rugby, Cricket, Formula 1
- **Public service broadcaster influence**: Very strong (BBC, ITV, Channel 4)

## 6. Regulatory Environment

### Broadcast Regulation
- **Licensing authorities**: Ofcom
- **Spectrum allocation**: DTT (Freeview), satellite (Astra, Eutelsat)
- **Must-carry rules**: Yes - public service channels must be carried
- **Local content quota**: European content quotas apply

### Consumer & Platform Regulation
- **Anti-piracy environment**: Strong enforcement
- **CAS/DRM compliance**: Mandatory for pay-TV
- **CI+ certification requirements**: Yes, via CI+ Consortium
- **Taxes on TV subscriptions**: VAT 20%, TV Licence fee for BBC
- **Data protection**: GDPR compliant

## 7. Pay-TV, OTT, and Advertising Market KPIs

### Pay-TV
- **Pay-TV ARPU**: ~£45-55/month
- **Pay-TV revenue growth rate**: ~1-2% annually
- **Number of major operators**: 3-4 (Sky UK, Virgin Media, BT TV, TalkTalk)
- **Churn rate**: ~12-15% annually

### Advertising
