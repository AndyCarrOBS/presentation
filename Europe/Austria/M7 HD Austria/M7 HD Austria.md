# TV Platform Operator: M7 HD Austria

## 1. Executive Summary

- **Overview of the operator's business**: M7 HD Austria is a satellite pay-TV platform in Austria, operated by M7 Group. The platform provides premium HD content including sports, movies, series, and entertainment channels via satellite delivery.

- **Key market position**: Satellite pay-TV platform in Austria, part of M7 Group with 3.6 million subscribers across European operations (2023)

- **Geographic footprint**: Nationwide coverage in Austria via satellite

- **Ownership structure**: Part of M7 Group (owned by Canal+ Group/Vivendi)

- **Overall growth/decline assessment**: Stable - M7 Group reported 3.6 million subscribers across European operations (2023). HD Austria had 125,000 customers in 2018 with 25% growth, but more recent data not disclosed. European satellite market stable.
