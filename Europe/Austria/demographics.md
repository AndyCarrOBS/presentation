# Austria - Demographics & Market Data

## 1. Core Population & Household Structure

### Population Basics
- **Total population**: ~8.97 million (2024)
- **Urban vs rural**: ~59.7% urban, ~40.3% rural
- **Population density**: ~107 people/km²
- **Household count**: ~4.1 million households
- **Average household size**: ~2.2 people

### Age Structure
- **0-14**: ~14%
- **15-24**: ~11%
- **25-44**: ~26%
- **45-64**: ~27%
- **65+**: ~22%
- **Median age**: ~44 years

### Household Types
- **Single-person households**: ~38%
- **Family households with children**: ~28%
- **Multi-generational homes**: ~8%
- **Socioeconomic segmentation**: ABC1 ~50%, C2DE ~50%

## 2. Economic Indicators

### Macro-economics
- **GDP (total)**: ~€450 billion (2024)
- **GDP per capita**: ~€50,000
- **GDP growth rate**: ~0.8% (2024)
- **Inflation rate**: ~3.2% (2024)
- **Disposable income per household**: ~€36,000/year
- **Cost of living index**: 102 (EU average = 100)

### Consumer Spend Capacity
- **Average telecom spend per household**: ~€55/month
- **Average entertainment/media spend**: ~€70/month
- **Credit card penetration**: ~80%
- **Digital payments adoption**: ~75%

- **Average broadband speed**: ~140 Mbps
- **FTTH availability**: ~45% (growing)
- **Mobile data penetration**: 4G/5G coverage ~98%
- **Mobile broadband ARPU**: ~€28/month

### Devices
- **Smart TV penetration**: ~65%
- **Streaming media players**: ~45%
- **Smartphone penetration**: ~90%
- **PC/tablet penetration**: ~82%
- **Console penetration**: ~40%

## 4. Television Access & Platform Penetration

### TV Platform Split
- **Total TV households**: ~4.2 million TV households (estimated)
- **Pay-TV households**: ~2-2.5 million (~50%)
- **Free-to-air households**: ~1.7-2.2 million (~50%)

### Market share by delivery type:
- **Satellite**: ~29% (growing)
- **Cable**: ~20% (growing)
- **IPTV/FTTH**: ~46% (internet-based TV, 2024)
- **DTT**: ~5%
- **OTT-only households**: ~15%

### Platform Technology & CA
- **CI+ penetration**: ~45% of pay-TV households
- **CAM vs STB share**: ~35% CAM, ~65% STB
- **Conditional Access systems**: Nagravision, Irdeto, Conax
- **Smartcard vs cardless CAS**: Mixed (smartcard dominant)
- **Operator-owned STBs vs retail devices**: ~70% operator STBs

### OTT & Streaming
- **SVOD penetration**: ~77% of Austrians use TV streaming services (2025)
- **AVOD penetration**: ~55% of households
- **Hybrid STB penetration**: ~50%
- **TV Everywhere usage**: ~40% of pay-TV subscribers
- **46% receive TV content over internet** (2024, +5% from previous year)

## 5. Language, Culture & Content Consumption

### Language
- **Primary language**: German
- **% bilingual population**: ~60% (English as second language)
- **Requirements**: German dubbing/subtitling preferred

### Content Preferences
- **Top genres**: News, sports, movies, drama series, documentaries
- **Local content importance**: High - Austrian/German content preferred
- **Sports rights**: Football (Bundesliga), Skiing, Formula 1
- **Public service broadcaster influence**: Very strong (ORF)

## 6. Regulatory Environment

### Broadcast Regulation
- **Licensing authorities**: KommAustria
- **Spectrum allocation**: DTT (MUX1-8), satellite (Astra, Eutelsat)
- **Must-carry rules**: Yes - public channels must be carried
- **Local content quota**: European content quotas apply

### Consumer & Platform Regulation
- **Anti-piracy environment**: Strong enforcement
- **CAS/DRM compliance**: Mandatory for pay-TV
- **CI+ certification requirements**: Yes, via CI+ Consortium
- **Taxes on TV subscriptions**: VAT 20%, ORF-Gebühr (broadcast fee)
- **Data protection**: GDPR compliant

## 7. Pay-TV, OTT, and Advertising Market KPIs

### Pay-TV
- **Pay-TV ARPU**: ~€35-45/month
- **Pay-TV revenue growth rate**: ~1-2% annually
- **Number of major operators**: 4-5 (Magenta TV, HD Austria, SimpliTV, ORF, M7)

### Advertising

- **Growth forecasts**: ~2-3% annually

## 9. Competitive Landscape

### Major Operators

**Magenta TV (Deutsche Telekom)**
- Platform type: IPTV, Cable
- Subscriber numbers: ~4.2M TV households (estimated)
- Market share: ~35% pay-TV
- Technology: Operator STBs (typically)
- Features: 4K, multi-device streaming

**HD Austria (M7 Group)**
- Platform type: Satellite
- Subscriber numbers: Growing
- Market share: ~8% pay-TV
- Technology: Smartcards, CI+ compatibility varies
- Features: Multi-channel packages

**SimpliTV**
- Platform type: Satellite, Terrestrial
- Subscriber numbers: Growing
- Market share: ~5% pay-TV
- Technology: CI+ CAM modules
- Features: Pay-lite model, HD channels

**ORF (Österreichischer Rundfunk)**
- Platform type: Satellite, Terrestrial
- Subscriber numbers: Near universal coverage
- Market share: Public broadcaster
- Technology: CI+ modules for encrypted satellite channels
- Features: Free-to-air + premium satellite
