# TV Platform Operator: SimpliTV Terrestrial

## 1. Executive Summary

- **Overview of the operator's business**: SimpliTV Terrestrial is a terrestrial pay-TV platform in Austria, operated by A1 Telekom Austria. The platform provides premium content via DTT (Digital Terrestrial Television), integrated with A1's telecommunications services.

- **Key market position**: Terrestrial pay-TV platform in Austria, part of A1 Group

- **Geographic footprint**: Nationwide coverage in Austria via terrestrial network

- **Ownership structure**: Part of A1 Group (Telekom Austria AG)

- **Overall growth/decline assessment**: Stable - Part of A1 Group which reported strong financial performance. Terrestrial platform provides universal coverage but faces competition from IPTV and satellite.
