# TV Platform Operator: Magenta TV Austria

## 1. Executive Summary

- **Overview of the operator's business**: Magenta TV Austria is the IPTV and cable TV service of Magenta Telekom (Deutsche Telekom Austria), offering TV content via IPTV (over broadband) and cable, integrated with Deutsche Telekom's broadband and mobile services in Austria.

- **Key market position**: Major IPTV/Cable provider in Austria, part of Austria's largest telecommunications company

- **Geographic footprint**: Nationwide coverage in Austria through Deutsche Telekom's extensive network

- **Ownership structure**: Part of Deutsche Telekom AG (German telecommunications company), operating as Magenta Telekom in Austria

- **Overall growth/decline assessment**: Growth - Revenue increased 2.5% in 2024 (€1,494 million from €1,458 million in 2023), demonstrating stable growth in Austrian market.
