# Lithuania - Demographics & Market Data

## 1. Core Population & Household Structure

### Population Basics
- **Total population**: ~2.89 million (2024), +4,800 (+0.2%) from previous year (immigration exceeded emigration by 23,100 despite negative natural change)
- **Urban vs rural**: ~68% urban, ~32% rural
- **Population density**: ~44 people/km²
- **Household count**: ~1.2 million households
- **Average household size**: ~2.4 people

### Age Structure
- **0-14**: ~15%
- **15-24**: ~11%
- **25-44**: ~26%
- **45-64**: ~28%
- **65+**: ~20%
- **Median age**: ~43 years

### Household Types
- **Single-person households**: ~34%
- **Family households with children**: ~36%
- **Multi-generational homes**: ~10%
- **Socioeconomic segmentation**: ABC1 ~40%, C2DE ~60%

## 2. Economic Indicators

### Macro-economics
- **GDP (total)**: ~€70 billion (2024)
- **GDP per capita**: ~€24,000
- **GDP growth rate**: ~2.5% (2024)
- **Inflation rate**: ~3.0% (2024)
- **Disposable income per household**: ~€21,000/year
- **Cost of living index**: 68 (EU average = 100)

### Consumer Spend Capacity
- **Average telecom spend per household**: ~€40/month
- **Average entertainment/media spend**: ~€52/month
- **Credit card penetration**: ~65%
- **Digital payments adoption**: ~75%

## 3. Connectivity & Device Penetration

### Broadband
- **Broadband penetration**: ~89.0% internet penetration (2024), ~2.41 million internet users
- **Average broadband speed**: ~130 Mbps
- **FTTH availability**: ~80.7% of total fixed broadband subscriptions are fiber (end 2024), ranks among top OECD countries for fiber penetration
- **Mobile data penetration**: 4G/5G coverage ~96%
- **Mobile broadband ARPU**: ~€17/month

### Devices
- **Smart TV penetration**: ~62%
- **Streaming media players**: ~44%
- **Smartphone penetration**: ~89%
- **PC/tablet penetration**: ~83%
- **Console penetration**: ~34%

## 4. Television Access & Platform Penetration

### TV Platform Split
- **Total TV households**: ~1.2 million
- **Pay-TV households**: ~720,000 (~60%)
- **Free-to-air households**: ~480,000 (~40%)
- **Average daily TV viewing time**: ~201 minutes (2024), younger audiences (13-29) reduced viewing

### Market share by delivery type:
- **Satellite**: ~25%
- **Cable**: ~30%
- **IPTV/FTTH**: ~35%
- **DTT**: ~10%
- **OTT-only households**: ~10%

### Platform Technology & CA
- **CI+ penetration**: ~40% of pay-TV households
- **CAM vs STB share**: ~30% CAM, ~70% STB
- **Conditional Access systems**: Conax, Irdeto, Nagravision
- **Smartcard vs cardless CAS**: Mixed (cardless growing)
- **Operator-owned STBs vs retail devices**: ~70% operator STBs

### OTT & Streaming
- **SVOD penetration**: ~52% of households
- **AVOD penetration**: ~46% of households
- **Hybrid STB penetration**: ~44%
- **TV Everywhere usage**: ~33% of pay-TV subscribers

## 5. Language, Culture & Content Consumption

### Language
- **Primary language**: Lithuanian
- **% bilingual population**: ~70% (English as second language)
- **Requirements**: Lithuanian subtitling preferred

### Content Preferences
- **Top genres**: News, sports, movies, drama series, documentaries
- **Local content importance**: High - Lithuanian content preferred
- **Sports rights**: Basketball, Football, Ice Hockey
- **Public service broadcaster influence**: Strong (LRT)

## 6. Regulatory Environment

### Broadcast Regulation
- **Licensing authorities**: RTCL (Radio and Television Commission of Lithuania)
- **Spectrum allocation**: DTT (MUX1-4), satellite (Eutelsat, SES)
- **Must-carry rules**: Yes - public channels must be carried
- **Local content quota**: European content quotas apply

### Consumer & Platform Regulation
- **Anti-piracy environment**: Moderate enforcement
- **CAS/DRM compliance**: Mandatory for pay-TV
- **CI+ certification requirements**: Yes, via CI+ Consortium
- **Taxes on TV subscriptions**: VAT 21%
- **Data protection**: GDPR compliant

## 7. Pay-TV, OTT, and Advertising Market KPIs

### Pay-TV
- **Pay-TV ARPU**: ~€24-32/month
- **Pay-TV revenue growth rate**: ~2-3% annually
- **Number of major operators**: 3-4 (Telia Lietuva, Bitė Lietuva, Cgates, Tele2 Lithuania)
- **Churn rate**: ~14-16% annually

### OTT/SVOD
- **Market size**: ~€85 million
- **Revenue/ARPU**: ~€8-10/month per service
- **Top players**: Netflix, HBO Max, Viaplay, Go3
- **Subscriber growth rates**: ~10-12% annually

### Advertising
- **TV advertising revenue**: ~€110 million
- **AVOD/CTV ad spend**: ~€25 million
- **Growth forecasts**: ~3-4% annually
- **Programmatic adoption**: ~36%

## 8. Market Forecasts

- **Pay-TV household forecast (5-10 years)**: Stable to slight growth (~0.5% annually)
- **OTT/SVOD penetration forecast**: Growth to ~68% by 2030
- **Broadband and FTTH growth**: Fiber to reach ~90% by 2030
- **Economic forecast**: Moderate growth (~2.5% GDP annually)
- **Population forecast**: Stable to slight growth (immigration offsetting natural decline)

### Major Operators

- Platform type: IPTV, Cable

- Market share: ~32% pay-TV
- Technology: Operator STBs, Telia TV platform
- Features: 4K, multi-device streaming

- Platform type: IPTV
- Subscriber numbers: Growing
- Market share: ~25% pay-TV
- Technology: Operator STBs
