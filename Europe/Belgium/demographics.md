# Belgium - Demographics & Market Data

## 1. Core Population & Household Structure

### Population Basics
- **Total population**: ~11.76 million (2024)
- **Urban vs rural**: ~98% urban, ~2% rural
- **Population density**: ~383 people/km²
- **Household count**: ~5.2 million households
- **Average household size**: ~2.3 people

### Age Structure
- **0-14**: ~16%
- **15-24**: ~12%
- **25-44**: ~26%
- **45-64**: ~27%
- **65+**: ~19%
- **Median age**: ~42 years

### Household Types
- **Single-person households**: ~35%
- **Family households with children**: ~32%
- **Multi-generational homes**: ~6%
- **Socioeconomic segmentation**: ABC1 ~48%, C2DE ~52%

## 2. Economic Indicators

### Macro-economics
- **GDP (total)**: ~€550 billion (2024)
- **GDP per capita**: ~€47,000
- **GDP growth rate**: ~1.0% (2024)
- **Inflation rate**: ~3.0% (2024)
- **Disposable income per household**: ~€35,000/year
- **Cost of living index**: 105 (EU average = 100)

### Consumer Spend Capacity
- **Average telecom spend per household**: ~€60/month
- **Average entertainment/media spend**: ~€75/month
- **Credit card penetration**: ~85%
- **Digital payments adoption**: ~80%

## 3. Connectivity & Device Penetration

### Broadband
- **Broadband penetration**: ~96% of households (Flemish Region, 2024), ~94% EU average
- **Average broadband speed**: ~160 Mbps
- **FTTH availability**: ~55% (growing)
- **Mobile data penetration**: 4G/5G coverage ~99%, ~90% mobile broadband adoption (2021)
- **Mobile broadband ARPU**: ~€27/month
- **Fixed broadband ≥100 Mbps**: ~67% (2022, above EU average of 55%)

### Devices
- **Smart TV penetration**: ~68%
- **Streaming media players**: ~48%
- **Smartphone penetration**: ~91%
- **PC/tablet penetration**: ~83%
- **Console penetration**: ~43%

## 4. Television Access & Platform Penetration

### TV Platform Split
- **Total TV households**: ~4.8 million TV households
- **Pay-TV households**: ~3-3.5 million (~65%)
- **Free-to-air households**: ~1.3-1.8 million (~35%)

### Market share by delivery type:
- **Satellite**: ~25%
- **Cable**: ~50% (very high cable penetration)
- **IPTV/FTTH**: ~20%
- **DTT**: ~5%
- **OTT-only households**: ~12%

### Platform Technology & CA
- **CI+ penetration**: ~55% of pay-TV households
- **CAM vs STB share**: ~40% CAM, ~60% STB
- **Conditional Access systems**: Nagravision, Irdeto
- **Smartcard vs cardless CAS**: Mixed
- **Operator-owned STBs vs retail devices**: ~75% operator STBs

### OTT & Streaming
- **SVOD penetration**: ~50% of households
- **AVOD penetration**: ~45% of households
- **Hybrid STB penetration**: ~50%
- **TV Everywhere usage**: ~38% of pay-TV subscribers
- **47% of population use internet to watch TV** (2024, up from 27% in 2018)

## 5. Language, Culture & Content Consumption

### Language
- **Primary language**: Dutch (Flanders), French (Wallonia), German (small region)
- **% bilingual population**: ~60% (multilingual country)
- **Requirements**: Content in Dutch/French/German depending on region

### Content Preferences
- **Top genres**: News, sports, movies, drama series, reality TV
- **Local content importance**: High - regional content preferred
- **Sports rights**: Football (Pro League), Cycling, Tennis
- **Public service broadcaster influence**: Strong (VRT, RTBF)

## 6. Regulatory Environment

### Broadcast Regulation
- **Licensing authorities**: VRT (Flanders), CSA (Wallonia)
- **Spectrum allocation**: DTT, satellite (Astra, Eutelsat)
- **Must-carry rules**: Yes - public channels must be carried
- **Local content quota**: European content quotas apply

### Consumer & Platform Regulation
- **Anti-piracy environment**: Strong enforcement
- **CAS/DRM compliance**: Mandatory for pay-TV
- **CI+ certification requirements**: Yes, via CI+ Consortium
- **Taxes on TV subscriptions**: VAT 21%
- **Data protection**: GDPR compliant

## 7. Pay-TV, OTT, and Advertising Market KPIs

### Pay-TV
- **Pay-TV ARPU**: ~€40-50/month
- **Pay-TV revenue growth rate**: ~1-2% annually
- **Number of major operators**: 3-4 (Telenet, Proximus Pickx, Voo, M7)

## 9. Competitive Landscape

### Major Operators

**Telenet**
- Platform type: Cable
- Subscriber numbers: ~4.8M TV households, >95% cable penetration
- Market share: ~40% pay-TV
- Technology: CI+ CAM modules, operator STBs
- Features: 4K, Gigabit broadband, triple-play bundles

**Proximus Pickx**
- Platform type: IPTV, OTT
- Subscriber numbers: ~1.41 million
- Market share: ~15% pay-TV
- Technology: Operator STBs (typically)
- Features: 4K, multi-device streaming

**Voo**
- Platform type: Cable
- Subscriber numbers: Growing
- Market share: ~8% pay-TV
- Technology: CI+ CAM modules
- Features: 4K, triple-play bundles

**M7 (Telesat, TV Vlaanderen)**
- Platform type: Satellite
- Subscriber numbers: ~25,200 (Dec 2023)
- Market share: ~1% pay-TV
- Technology: Smartcards, CI+ compatibility varies
- Features: Multi-channel packages
