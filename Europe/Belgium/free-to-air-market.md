# Belgium Free-to-Air TV Market Analysis

## Executive Summary

Belgium's free-to-air TV market is unique with high cable penetration (95% coverage) and regional differences between Flanders and Wallonia. The market is characterized by strong public service broadcasters (VRT in Flanders, RTBF in Wallonia), major commercial broadcasters (VTM, La Une), and a growing ecosystem of local OTT services that hero Belgian content.

---

## 1. Free-to-Air Platform Distribution

### 1.1 Platform Breakdown (2024-2025)

**Total TV Households: ~4.8 million (estimated)**

#### • Cable Reception (Free-to-Air Tier)
- **Households**: 95% cable coverage (significantly higher than EU average of 45.1%)
- **Year-on-year change**: Declining subscriptions (Telenet -18,000 Q1 2024, Proximus -15,000 Q1 2024)
- **Primary delivery method**: DOCSIS cable networks (Telenet, Proximus, Voo)
- **Coverage**: Near universal (95% coverage)
- **Key characteristics**:
  - Basic cable tier includes free-to-air channels
  - Must-carry rules ensure VRT/RTBF availability
  - Highest cable penetration in Europe
  - Declining subscriptions due to streaming

#### • Satellite Reception
- **Households**: Data not publicly disclosed separately
- **Primary delivery method**: Satellite (Astra, Eutelsat)
- **Coverage**: Near universal
- **Key characteristics**: 
  - Free-to-air channels available without subscription
  - Subscription services: TV Vlaanderen (Flemish), TéléSAT (French-speaking)
  - HD channels available

#### • Digital Terrestrial Television (DTT)
- **Flanders**: **Discontinued** (VRT ceased DVB-T December 1, 2018, only 1% usage)
- **Wallonia/Brussels**: **Active** (RTBF continues DVB-T2 free-to-air)
- **Households**: RTBF DVB-T2 available in Brussels and Wallonia
- **Primary delivery method**: DVB-T2 terrestrial network
- **Coverage**: Brussels and Wallonia only
- **Key characteristics**:
  - Free-to-air HD channels (Wallonia/Brussels)
  - No subscription required
  - Regional differences (Flanders discontinued, Wallonia active)
  - Antenne TV (subscription DVB-T2) discontinued September 1, 2024

#### • Internet-Based Reception (IPTV/OTT)
- **Households**: Growing trend
- **Primary delivery method**: IPTV, streaming apps, smart TV apps
- **Key characteristics**:
  - IPTV services (Proximus Pickx)
  - Streaming apps
  - Hybrid consumption (linear + streaming)
  - Growing internet-based consumption

### 1.2 Platform Trends

**Key Developments:**
- **Cable**: Highest penetration in Europe (95%) but declining subscriptions
- **DTT Regional Differences**: Flanders discontinued (2018), Wallonia active (RTBF DVB-T2)
- **Satellite**: Subscription services (TV Vlaanderen, TéléSAT)
- **Internet**: Growing IPTV and streaming consumption
- **Regulatory**: Flemish Government mandates pre-installed local TV apps on smart TVs

## 2. Main Free-to-Air Broadcasters

### 2.1 Public Service Broadcasters

#### • **VRT (Vlaamse Radio- en Televisieomroep)**
- **Market Share**: Leading broadcaster in Flanders
- **Position**: Flemish public service broadcaster
- **Main Channels**:
  - **Eén** - Main Flemish channel
  - **Canvas** - Culture and documentaries
  - **Ketnet** - Kids channel
  - **VRT NWS** - News channel
- **Reach**: Near universal coverage in Flanders (public service broadcaster)
- **Content Focus**: News, entertainment, documentaries, cultural content, kids content
- **Funding**: License fee + advertising (limited)
- **Key Characteristics**:
  - Strong news and information programming
  - Regional content production
  - High-quality documentaries
  - Cultural content
  - Kids programming
  - Must-carry on all platforms
  - DTT discontinued December 1, 2018 (low usage, 1%)
- **OTT Service**: **VRT Max** - Leading Flemish OTT service

#### • **RTBF (Radio Télévision Belge Francophone)**
- **Market Share**: Leading broadcaster in Wallonia
- **Position**: French-speaking public service broadcaster
- **Main Channels**:
  - **La Une** - Main French-speaking channel
  - **Tipik** - Entertainment
  - **La Trois** - Culture and documentaries
- **Reach**: Near universal coverage in Wallonia (public service broadcaster)
- **Content Focus**: News, entertainment, documentaries, cultural content
- **Funding**: License fee + advertising (limited)
- **Key Characteristics**:
  - Strong news and information programming
  - Regional content production
  - High-quality documentaries
  - Cultural content
  - Must-carry on all platforms
  - DVB-T2 free-to-air available in Brussels and Wallonia
- **OTT Service**: **RTBF Auvio** - Leading French-speaking OTT service

### 2.2 Commercial Broadcasters

#### • **VTM (Flanders)**
- **Market Share**: Leading commercial broadcaster in Flanders
- **Position**: Flemish commercial broadcaster
- **Main Channels**:
  - **VTM** - Main Flemish commercial channel

  - **La Une** - Main French-speaking commercial channel

  - **Entertainment**: VRT series, documentaries, cultural content

  - Free access (no subscription)

## 5. Platform Technology & Delivery

### 5.1 Cable (DOCSIS)
- **Free-to-Air Channels**: 100+ channels
- **HD Channels**: Majority available in HD
- **Coverage**: 95% of households (highest in Europe)
- **Technology**: DOCSIS 3.0/3.1, MPEG-4
- **Providers**: Telenet, Proximus, Voo

### 5.2 Satellite
- **Free-to-Air Channels**: 50+ channels
- **HD Channels**: Majority available in HD
- **Coverage**: 98%+ population coverage
- **Technology**: DVB-S2, MPEG-4, HEVC
- **Subscription Services**: TV Vlaanderen (Flemish), TéléSAT (French-speaking)

### 5.3 Digital Terrestrial (DVB-T2)
- **Flanders**: Discontinued (December 1, 2018)
- **Wallonia/Brussels**: Active (RTBF DVB-T2 free-to-air)
- **Free-to-Air Channels**: RTBF channels (La Une, Tipik, La Trois)
- **HD Channels**: Available in HD
- **Coverage**: Brussels and Wallonia
- **Technology**: DVB-T2, HEVC, MPEG-4

### 5.4 Internet-Based (IPTV/OTT)
- **Catch-up Services**: VRT Max, RTBF Auvio, VTM GO
- **Live Streaming**: Live streams of free-to-air channels
- **IPTV**: Proximus Pickx
- **Smart TV Apps**: Available on smart TVs (VRT Max pre-installed mandate)
- **Streaming Devices**: Available on streaming media players
- **Technology**: HLS, DASH, DRM (for premium content)

- **DTT Regional**: Flanders discontinued (low usage), Wallonia active
