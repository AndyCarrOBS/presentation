# TV Platform Operator: Voo

## 1. Executive Summary

- **Overview of the operator's business**: Voo is a cable operator in Belgium, primarily serving the Wallonia region (French-speaking). The company offers TV, broadband internet, and telephony services via cable network. Voo was acquired by Orange Belgium in 2022.

- **Key market position**: Cable operator in Belgium, primarily Wallonia region, part of Orange Belgium

- **Geographic footprint**: Primarily Wallonia region, Belgium (cable network coverage)

- **Ownership structure**: Part of Orange Belgium (acquired in 2022)

- **Overall growth/decline assessment**: Growth - Cable customer base grew 3.5% YoY in 2024, totaling 1,021,000 customers. Orange Belgium total revenue +1.7% YoY in 2024 (€1,993.7 million). Successful operational integration with Orange Belgium.
