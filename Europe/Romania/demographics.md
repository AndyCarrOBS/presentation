# Romania - Demographics & Market Data

## 1. Core Population & Household Structure

### Population Basics
- **Total population**: ~19.73 million (2024)
- **Urban vs rural**: ~54.8% urban, ~45.2% rural
- **Population density**: ~83 people/km²
- **Household count**: ~7.6 million households
- **Average household size**: ~2.6 people

### Age Structure
- **0-14**: ~15%
- **15-24**: ~11%
- **25-44**: ~28%
- **45-64**: ~28%
- **65+**: ~18%
- **Median age**: ~43 years

### Household Types
- **Single-person households**: ~20%
- **Family households with children**: ~50%
- **Multi-generational homes**: ~20%
- **Socioeconomic segmentation**: ABC1 ~30%, C2DE ~70%

## 2. Economic Indicators

### Macro-economics
- **GDP (total)**: ~€300 billion (2024)
- **GDP per capita**: ~€15,000
- **GDP growth rate**: ~3.5% (2024)
- **Inflation rate**: ~5.0% (2024)
- **Disposable income per household**: ~€15,000/year
- **Cost of living index**: 45 (EU average = 100)

### Consumer Spend Capacity
- **Average telecom spend per household**: ~€30/month
- **Average entertainment/media spend**: ~€35/month
- **Credit card penetration**: ~45%
- **Digital payments adoption**: ~55%

## 3. Connectivity & Device Penetration

### Broadband
- **Broadband penetration**: ~88.6% of households (2024), 92.5% urban, 83.2% rural
- **Average broadband speed**: ~150 Mbps (95% of connections >100 Mbps, 35% ≥1 Gbps)
- **FTTH availability**: ~60% (growing)
- **Mobile data penetration**: 4G/5G coverage ~85% (21.3 million mobile internet connections)
- **Mobile broadband ARPU**: ~€12/month

### Devices
- **Smart TV penetration**: ~50%
- **Streaming media players**: ~30%
- **Smartphone penetration**: ~75%
- **PC/tablet penetration**: ~65%
- **Console penetration**: ~25%

## 4. Television Access & Platform Penetration

### TV Platform Split
- **Total TV households**: ~7.6 million
- **Pay-TV households**: ~7.8 million TV service subscribers (2024)
- **Free-to-air households**: ~2-3 million

### Market share by delivery type:
- **Satellite**: ~35% (declining -12% in 2024)
- **Cable**: ~55% (growing +2% in 2024)
- **IPTV/FTTH**: ~8%
- **DTT**: ~2%
- **OTT-only households**: ~5%

### Platform Technology & CA
- **CI+ penetration**: ~40% of pay-TV households
- **CAM vs STB share**: ~30% CAM, ~70% STB
- **Conditional Access systems**: Nagravision, Irdeto, Conax
- **Smartcard vs cardless CAS**: Smartcard dominant
- **Operator-owned STBs vs retail devices**: ~65% operator STBs

### OTT & Streaming
- **SVOD penetration**: ~35% of households
- **AVOD penetration**: ~30% of households
- **Hybrid STB penetration**: ~30%
- **TV Everywhere usage**: ~20% of pay-TV subscribers

## 5. Language, Culture & Content Consumption

### Language
- **Primary language**: Romanian
- **% bilingual population**: ~25% (English as second language)
- **Requirements**: Romanian dubbing/subtitling preferred

### Content Preferences
- **Top genres**: News, sports, movies, drama series, reality TV, telenovelas
- **Local content importance**: High - Romanian content preferred
- **Sports rights**: Football (Liga 1), Handball, Tennis
- **Public service broadcaster influence**: Strong (TVR)

## 6. Regulatory Environment

### Broadcast Regulation
- **Licensing authorities**: CNA (Consiliul Național al Audiovizualului)
- **Spectrum allocation**: DTT, satellite (Eutelsat, SES)
- **Must-carry rules**: Yes - public channels must be carried
- **Local content quota**: European content quotas apply

### Consumer & Platform Regulation
- **Anti-piracy environment**: Moderate enforcement
- **CAS/DRM compliance**: Mandatory for pay-TV
- **CI+ certification requirements**: Yes, via CI+ Consortium
- **Taxes on TV subscriptions**: VAT 19%
- **Data protection**: GDPR compliant

## 7. Pay-TV, OTT, and Advertising Market KPIs

### Pay-TV
- **Pay-TV ARPU**: ~€20-30/month
- **Pay-TV revenue growth rate**: ~3-4% annually
- **Number of major operators**: 4-5 (Digi Communications, Vodafone Romania, Orange, Telekom Romania, Focus Sat)
- **Churn rate**: ~18-20% annually

### OTT/SVOD
- **Market size**: ~€200 million
- **Revenue/ARPU**: ~€6-8/month per service
- **Top players**: Netflix, HBO Max, Amazon Prime, Digi Online, Voyo
- **Subscriber growth rates**: ~15-18% annually

### Advertising
- **TV advertising revenue**: ~€400 million
- **AVOD/CTV ad spend**: ~€80 million
- **Growth forecasts**: ~4-5% annually
- **Programmatic adoption**: ~30%

## 8. Market Forecasts

## 9. Competitive Landscape

### Major Operators

**Digi Communications (RCS&RDS)**
- Platform type: Cable, Satellite, IPTV
- Subscriber numbers: ~6.6 million cable (2022), 69% cable TV market share
- Market share: ~45% pay-TV
- Technology: CI+ CAM (launched Nov 2013), operator STBs
- Features: 4K, DigiTV cable and satellite platforms, triple-play

**Vodafone Romania (UPC)**
- Platform type: Cable, IPTV
- Subscriber numbers: ~1-2 million
- Market share: ~12% pay-TV
- Technology: CI+ CAM (launched April 2012), operator STBs
- Features: 4K, triple-play bundles

**Orange Romania**
- Platform type: IPTV, OTT
- Subscriber numbers: ~500,000-1 million
- Market share: ~8% pay-TV
- Technology: Operator STBs, Orange Home TV IPTV
- Features: 4K, catch-up TV, multi-device streaming

**Telekom România**
- Platform type: Satellite, IPTV
- Subscriber numbers: >4 million subscriptions (2022)
- Market share: ~15% pay-TV
- Technology: Telekom TV satellite platform, IPTV services
- Features: Multi-platform offering

**Focus Sat (M7 Group)**
- Platform type: Satellite
- Subscriber numbers: ~1.2 million satellite subscribers (2022)
- Market share: ~8% pay-TV
- Technology: Smartcards, CI+ compatibility varies
- Features: Multi-channel packages
