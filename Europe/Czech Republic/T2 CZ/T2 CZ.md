# TV Platform Operator: T2 CZ

## 1. Executive Summary

- **Overview of the operator's business**: T2 CZ is a terrestrial pay-TV platform in the Czech Republic, providing pay television channels on Czech Republic's digital terrestrial network. The platform offers premium content via DTT (Digital Terrestrial Television).

- **Key market position**: Terrestrial pay-TV platform in Czech Republic

- **Geographic footprint**: Nationwide coverage in Czech Republic via terrestrial network

- **Ownership structure**: Data not publicly disclosed

- **Overall growth/decline assessment**: Decline - Market shift toward IPTV and cable (46% of households) vs terrestrial (41%) in 2024. Terrestrial TV reception declining as consumers prefer interactive IPTV services with catch-up viewing and ad-skipping capabilities.

## 4. Growth Assessment

### 4.1 Market Position

- Ranking vs. competitors: Terrestrial pay-TV platform, smaller market presence
- Strength in broadband, mobile, and TV: Focus on TV services via terrestrial delivery
- Differentiators: Terrestrial delivery provides universal coverage

### 4.2 Growth Indicators

- Subscriber trends across FTTH, broadband, TV, and mobile: Declining terrestrial reception share
- ARPU trends: Data not publicly disclosed
- Network upgrades: Limited (terrestrial platform)
- New service launches: Limited interactive services compared to IPTV

### 4.3 Decline Indicators

- Cord-cutting pressure: Significant pressure from OTT competitors and IPTV
- Regulatory or pricing pressure: Market shift toward IPTV/cable
- Content rights losses: Standard content portfolio maintained
- Competitive erosion from OTT: Competition from Netflix, Disney+, Amazon Prime Video, IPTV services with interactive features

### **Overall Assessment**

- **Operator Status:** Decline

- **Drivers Behind the Trend:** Market shift toward IPTV and cable (46% of households) vs terrestrial (41%) in 2024. Terrestrial TV reception declining as consumers prefer interactive IPTV services with catch-up viewing and ad-skipping capabilities. Average monthly pay-TV expenditure increased to CZK 424 in 2024, but consumers are choosing IPTV/cable over terrestrial. Czech telecom and pay-TV service revenue projected to grow at 2.8% CAGR from 2023-2028, driven by mobile data and fixed broadband, not terrestrial TV. Terrestrial platform lacks interactive features that attract viewers to IPTV.
