# Czech Republic - Demographics & Market Data

## 1. Core Population & Household Structure

### Population Basics
- **Total population**: ~10.50 million (2024)
- **Urban vs rural**: ~74.7% urban, ~25.3% rural
- **Population density**: ~134 people/km²
- **Household count**: ~4.5 million households
- **Average household size**: ~2.3 people

### Age Structure
- **0-14**: ~15%
- **15-24**: ~11%
- **25-44**: ~26%
- **45-64**: ~28%
- **65+**: ~20%
- **Median age**: ~43 years

### Household Types
- **Single-person households**: ~32%
- **Family households with children**: ~38%
- **Multi-generational homes**: ~10%
- **Socioeconomic segmentation**: ABC1 ~40%, C2DE ~60%

## 2. Economic Indicators

### Macro-economics
- **GDP (total)**: ~€280 billion (2024)
- **GDP per capita**: ~€27,000
- **GDP growth rate**: ~2.0% (2024)
- **Inflation rate**: ~3.5% (2024)
- **Disposable income per household**: ~€22,000/year
- **Cost of living index**: 70 (EU average = 100)

### Consumer Spend Capacity
- **Average telecom spend per household**: ~€40/month
- **Average entertainment/media spend**: ~€50/month
- **Credit card penetration**: ~60%
- **Digital payments adoption**: ~65%

## 3. Connectivity & Device Penetration

### Broadband
- **Broadband penetration**: ~92.8% internet penetration (2024), ~38-40 fixed broadband subscriptions per 100 people (2023)
- **Average broadband speed**: ~120 Mbps
- **FTTH availability**: ~50% (growing)
- **Mobile data penetration**: 4G/5G coverage ~95% (11.5 million active mobile internet subscriptions)
- **Mobile broadband ARPU**: ~€18/month
- **15.5 million mobile SIM cards** (mobile subscriptions nearly 3x fixed broadband)

### Devices
- **Smart TV penetration**: ~48.4% of households (2024, up from 43.8% in 2022)
- **Streaming media players**: ~35%
- **Smartphone penetration**: ~85%
- **PC/tablet penetration**: ~75%
- **Console penetration**: ~32%

## 4. Television Access & Platform Penetration

### TV Platform Split
- **Total TV households**: ~4.5 million
- **Pay-TV households**: ~3-3.5 million (~70%)
- **Free-to-air households**: ~1-1.5 million (~30%)

### Market share by delivery type:
- **Satellite**: ~17.0% (DVB-S, Q4 2022)
- **Cable**: ~40.7% combined (CATV + IPTV, Q4 2022)
- **IPTV/FTTH**: Part of 40.7% combined
- **DTT**: ~54.5% (DVB-T, Q4 2022)
- **OTT-only households**: ~10%

### Platform Technology & CA
- **CI+ penetration**: ~45% of pay-TV households
- **CAM vs STB share**: ~35% CAM, ~65% STB
- **Conditional Access systems**: Nagravision, Irdeto, Conax
- **Smartcard vs cardless CAS**: Smartcard dominant
- **Operator-owned STBs vs retail devices**: ~65% operator STBs

### OTT & Streaming
- **SVOD penetration**: ~40% of households
- **AVOD penetration**: ~35% of households
- **Hybrid STB penetration**: ~40%
- **TV Everywhere usage**: ~25% of pay-TV subscribers
- **86% of Czechs watch TV at least once a week** (2024), average 3h 36min daily

## 5. Language, Culture & Content Consumption

### Language
- **Primary language**: Czech
- **% bilingual population**: ~30% (English as second language)
- **Requirements**: Czech dubbing/subtitling preferred

### Content Preferences
- **Top genres**: News, sports, movies, drama series, reality TV
- **Local content importance**: High - Czech content preferred
- **Sports rights**: Football, Ice Hockey, Tennis
- **Public service broadcaster influence**: Strong (Česká televize)

## 6. Regulatory Environment

### Broadcast Regulation
- **Licensing authorities**: RRTV (Rada pro rozhlasové a televizní vysílání)
- **Spectrum allocation**: DTT (MUX1-4), satellite (Eutelsat, SES)
- **Must-carry rules**: Yes - public channels must be carried
- **Local content quota**: European content quotas apply

### Consumer & Platform Regulation
- **Anti-piracy environment**: Moderate enforcement
- **CAS/DRM compliance**: Mandatory for pay-TV
- **CI+ certification requirements**: Yes, via CI+ Consortium
- **Taxes on TV subscriptions**: VAT 21%
- **Data protection**: GDPR compliant

## 7. Pay-TV, OTT, and Advertising Market KPIs

### Pay-TV
- **Pay-TV ARPU**: ~€25-35/month
- **Pay-TV revenue growth rate**: ~2-3% annually
- **Number of major operators**: 4-5 (T-Mobile Czech Republic, Vodafone Czech Republic, O2 Czech Republic, M7 Skylink, UPC)
- **Churn rate**: ~15-18% annually

### OTT/SVOD
- **Market size**: ~€300 million
- **Revenue/ARPU**: ~€7-9/month per service
- **Top players**: Netflix, HBO Max, Voyo, O2 TV, Oneplay
- **Subscriber growth rates**: ~12-15% annually

### Advertising
- **TV advertising revenue**: ~€600 million
- **AVOD/CTV ad spend**: ~€120 million
- **Growth forecasts**: ~3-4% annually
- **Programmatic adoption**: ~38%

## 8. Market Forecasts

## 9. Competitive Landscape

### Major Operators

**T-Mobile Czech Republic (Magenta TV)**
- Platform type: IPTV, OTT
- Subscriber numbers: Growing
- Market share: ~20% pay-TV
- Technology: Magenta TV IPTV service, over 170 channels in 4K
- Features: 4K, rebranded from T-Mobile TV (2023), multi-device

**Vodafone Czech Republic**
- Platform type: IPTV, OTT
- Subscriber numbers: Growing (acquired UPC 2019)
- Market share: ~18% pay-TV
- Technology: Vodafone TV IPTV service, over 100 channels
- Features: 4K, multi-device streaming

**O2 Czech Republic**
- Platform type: IPTV, OTT
- Subscriber numbers: Merged with Voyo to create Oneplay (2025)
- Market share: ~15% pay-TV
- Technology: O2 TV IPTV service, Oneplay platform
- Features: 4K, merged streaming/VOD service

**M7 (Skylink CZ)**
- Platform type: Satellite
- Subscriber numbers: ~1 million (2010)
- Market share: ~12% pay-TV
- Technology: Smartcards, CI+ compatibility varies
- Features: Czech and Slovak TV stations, multi-channel packages

**UPC (now Vodafone)**
- Platform type: Cable
- Subscriber numbers: Acquired by Vodafone (2019)
- Market share: ~10% pay-TV
- Technology: CI+ CAM modules typically required
- Features: Premium cable services
