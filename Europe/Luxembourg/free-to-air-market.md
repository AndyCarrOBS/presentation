# Luxembourg Free-to-Air TV Market Analysis

## Executive Summary

Luxembourg's free-to-air TV market is small but diverse, with access via DTT, satellite, and cable platforms. The market is characterized by RTL (Luxembourg's public broadcaster), access to neighboring countries' channels (Germany, France, Belgium), and limited local OTT services.

---

## 1. Free-to-Air Platform Distribution

### 1.1 Platform Breakdown (2024-2025)

**Total TV Households: Data not publicly disclosed (small market)**

#### • Satellite Reception
- **Households**: Data not publicly disclosed
- **Primary delivery method**: Astra satellite (19.2° East, managed by SES - Luxembourg-based)
- **Coverage**: Near universal
- **Key characteristics**: 
  - Free-to-air channels available without subscription
  - HD/UHD channels available
  - Access to Luxembourg, German, French, Belgian channels
  - SES (Luxembourg-based) manages satellite fleet including Astra 1P (launched June 2024)
  - Extended partnerships: RTL Deutschland-SES (July 2024), ProSiebenSat.1-SES (February 2025), Warner Bros. Discovery-SES (November 2024)

#### • Digital Terrestrial Television (DTT)
- **Households**: Data not publicly disclosed
- **Primary delivery method**: DVB-T2 terrestrial network
- **Coverage**: Nationwide
- **Key characteristics**:
  - Free-to-air HD channels
  - No subscription required
  - Limited channel selection
  - RTL 4 (Dutch channel) available at 498 MHz in SD

#### • Cable Reception (Free-to-Air Tier)
- **Households**: Data not publicly disclosed
- **Primary delivery method**: DOCSIS cable networks
- **Coverage**: Urban areas
- **Key characteristics**:
  - Basic cable tier includes free-to-air channels
  - Access to Luxembourg, German, French, Belgian channels
  - RTL 4 and RTL9 available via cable

### 1.2 Platform Trends

**Key Developments:**
- **Satellite**: Significant platform (SES Luxembourg-based, Astra 1P launched June 2024)
- **DTT**: Available but limited channel selection
- **Cable**: Available in urban areas
- **Cross-Border**: Access to neighboring countries' channels (Germany, France, Belgium)

**Unique Market Characteristics:**
- Small market size
- Cross-border channel access (Germany, France, Belgium)
- SES (Luxembourg-based) satellite operator
- Multi-language content (Luxembourgish, French, German)
