# Luxembourg - Demographics & Market Data

## 1. Core Population & Household Structure

### Population Basics
- **Total population**: ~677,000 (2024)
- **Urban vs rural**: ~90% urban, ~10% rural
- **Population density**: ~262 people/km²
- **Household count**: ~250,000 households
- **Average household size**: ~2.7 people

### Age Structure
- **0-14**: ~18.2% (0-4: 5.1%, 5-12: 8.4%, 13-17: 5.3%)
- **15-24**: ~7.8% (18-24)
- **25-44**: ~30.4% (25-34: 14.9%, 35-44: 15.5%)
- **45-64**: ~27.6% (45-54: 14.3%, 55-64: 13.1%)
- **65+**: ~15.5%
- **Median age**: ~40 years

### Household Types
- **Single-person households**: ~32%
- **Family households with children**: ~42%
- **Multi-generational homes**: ~8%
- **Socioeconomic segmentation**: ABC1 ~60%, C2DE ~40%

## 2. Economic Indicators

### Macro-economics
- **GDP (total)**: ~€90 billion (2024)
- **GDP per capita**: ~€133,000
- **GDP growth rate**: ~2.0% (2024)
- **Inflation rate**: ~3.0% (2024)
- **Disposable income per household**: ~€85,000/year
- **Cost of living index**: 120 (EU average = 100)

### Consumer Spend Capacity
- **Average telecom spend per household**: ~€80/month
- **Average entertainment/media spend**: ~€120/month
- **Credit card penetration**: ~95%
- **Digital payments adoption**: ~90%

## 3. Connectivity & Device Penetration

### Broadband
- **Broadband penetration**: ~99.0% of population are internet users (2024), ~98% of households have internet access
- **Average broadband speed**: ~200 Mbps
- **FTTH availability**: ~75% (very high coverage)
- **Mobile data penetration**: 4G/5G coverage ~99%
- **Mobile broadband ARPU**: ~€45/month

### Devices
- **Smart TV penetration**: ~78%
- **Streaming media players**: ~62%
- **Smartphone penetration**: ~96%
- **PC/tablet penetration**: ~94%
- **Console penetration**: ~48%

## 4. Television Access & Platform Penetration

### TV Platform Split
- **Total TV households**: ~250,000
- **Pay-TV households**: ~175,000 (~70%)
- **Free-to-air households**: ~75,000 (~30%)

### Market share by delivery type:
- **Satellite**: ~30%
- **Cable**: ~35%
- **IPTV/FTTH**: ~25%
- **DTT**: ~10%
- **OTT-only households**: ~15%

### Platform Technology & CA
- **CI+ penetration**: ~50% of pay-TV households
- **CAM vs STB share**: ~35% CAM, ~65% STB
- **Conditional Access systems**: Nagravision, Irdeto, Viaccess
- **Smartcard vs cardless CAS**: Mixed (smartcard dominant)
- **Operator-owned STBs vs retail devices**: ~70% operator STBs

### OTT & Streaming
- **SVOD penetration**: ~72% of households
- **AVOD penetration**: ~60% of households
- **Hybrid STB penetration**: ~58%
- **TV Everywhere usage**: ~50% of pay-TV subscribers

## 5. Language, Culture & Content Consumption

### Language
- **Primary language**: Luxembourgish, French, German (all official)
- **% bilingual population**: ~95% (multilingual country)
- **Requirements**: Content in Luxembourgish/French/German

### Content Preferences
- **Top genres**: News, sports, movies, drama series, documentaries
- **Local content importance**: High - Luxembourgish content preferred
- **Sports rights**: Football, Cycling
- **Public service broadcaster influence**: Strong (RTL)

## 6. Regulatory Environment

### Broadcast Regulation
- **Licensing authorities**: ALIA (Autorité luxembourgeoise indépendante de l'audiovisuel)
- **Spectrum allocation**: DTT, satellite (Astra, Eutelsat)
- **Must-carry rules**: Yes - public channels must be carried
- **Local content quota**: European content quotas apply

### Consumer & Platform Regulation
- **Anti-piracy environment**: Strong enforcement
- **CAS/DRM compliance**: Mandatory for pay-TV
- **CI+ certification requirements**: Yes, via CI+ Consortium
- **Taxes on TV subscriptions**: VAT 17%
- **Data protection**: GDPR compliant

## 7. Pay-TV, OTT, and Advertising Market KPIs

### Pay-TV
- **Pay-TV ARPU**: ~€60-70/month
- **Pay-TV revenue growth rate**: ~1-2% annually
- **Number of major operators**: 2-3 (POST Luxembourg, Tango, Eltrona)

### Advertising
