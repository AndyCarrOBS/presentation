# TV Platform Operator: M7 Telesat (Luxembourg)

## 1. Executive Summary

- **Overview of the operator's business**: M7 Telesat is a satellite pay-TV platform in Luxembourg, operated by M7 Group. The platform provides premium content including sports, movies, series, and entertainment channels via satellite delivery.

- **Key market position**: Satellite pay-TV platform in Luxembourg, part of M7 Group with 3.6 million subscribers across European operations (2023)

- **Geographic footprint**: Nationwide coverage in Luxembourg via satellite

- **Ownership structure**: Part of M7 Group (owned by Canal+ Group/Vivendi)

- **Overall growth/decline assessment**: Stable - M7 Group reported 3.6 million subscribers across European operations (2023). M7 Group revenue €318.9 million (2018). Luxembourg-specific metrics not disclosed separately.
