# Albania - Demographics & Market Data

## 1. Core Population & Household Structure

### Population Basics
- **Total population**: ~2.78 million (2024)
- **Urban vs rural**: ~63% urban, ~37% rural
- **Population density**: ~97 people/km²
- **Household count**: ~720,000 households
- **Average household size**: ~3.9 people

### Age Structure
- **0-14**: ~17%
- **15-24**: ~15%
- **25-44**: ~28%
- **45-64**: ~24%
- **65+**: ~16%
- **Median age**: ~38 years

### Household Types
- **Single-person households**: ~15%
- **Family households with children**: ~55%
- **Multi-generational homes**: ~25%
- **Socioeconomic segmentation**: ABC1 ~25%, C2DE ~75%

## 2. Economic Indicators

### Macro-economics
- **GDP (total)**: ~€18 billion (2024)
- **GDP per capita**: ~€6,500
- **GDP growth rate**: ~3.5% (2024)
- **Inflation rate**: ~4.5% (2024)
- **Disposable income per household**: ~€8,000/year
- **Cost of living index**: 35 (EU average = 100)

### Consumer Spend Capacity
- **Average telecom spend per household**: ~€25/month
- **Average entertainment/media spend**: ~€30/month
- **Credit card penetration**: ~35%
- **Digital payments adoption**: ~45%

## 3. Connectivity & Device Penetration

### Broadband
- **Broadband penetration**: ~92% of households have fixed internet access (end 2024, +4pp from previous year)
- **Average broadband speed**: ~80 Mbps
- **FTTH availability**: ~40% (growing)
- **Mobile data penetration**: 4G/5G coverage ~90%
- **Mobile broadband ARPU**: ~€10/month
- **Fixed-line internet service subscribers**: ~693,000 (end 2024, +10% from previous year)

### Devices
- **Smart TV penetration**: ~45%
- **Streaming media players**: ~25%
- **Smartphone penetration**: ~75%
- **PC/tablet penetration**: ~65%
- **Console penetration**: ~20%

## 4. Television Access & Platform Penetration

### TV Platform Split
- **Total TV households**: ~720,000
- **Pay-TV households**: ~400,000 (~55%)
- **Free-to-air households**: ~320,000 (~45%)

### Market share by delivery type:
- **Satellite**: ~40%
- **Cable**: ~30%
- **IPTV/FTTH**: ~20%
- **DTT**: ~10%
- **OTT-only households**: ~8%

### Platform Technology & CA
- **CI+ penetration**: ~30% of pay-TV households
- **CAM vs STB share**: ~25% CAM, ~75% STB
- **Conditional Access systems**: Nagravision, Irdeto, Conax
- **Smartcard vs cardless CAS**: Smartcard dominant
- **Operator-owned STBs vs retail devices**: ~70% operator STBs

### OTT & Streaming
- **SVOD penetration**: ~30% of households
- **AVOD penetration**: ~25% of households
- **Hybrid STB penetration**: ~25%
- **TV Everywhere usage**: ~18% of pay-TV subscribers

## 5. Language, Culture & Content Consumption

### Language
- **Primary language**: Albanian
- **% bilingual population**: ~40% (English as second language)
- **Requirements**: Albanian dubbing/subtitling preferred

### Content Preferences
- **Top genres**: News, sports, movies, drama series, reality TV
- **Local content importance**: Very high - Albanian content preferred
- **Sports rights**: Football, Basketball
- **Public service broadcaster influence**: Strong (RTSH)

## 6. Regulatory Environment

### Broadcast Regulation
- **Licensing authorities**: AMA (Audiovisual Media Authority)
- **Spectrum allocation**: DTT, satellite (Eutelsat, SES)
- **Must-carry rules**: Yes - public channels must be carried
- **Local content quota**: European content quotas apply

### Consumer & Platform Regulation
- **Anti-piracy environment**: Moderate enforcement
- **CAS/DRM compliance**: Mandatory for pay-TV
- **CI+ certification requirements**: Yes, via CI+ Consortium
- **Taxes on TV subscriptions**: VAT 20%
- **Data protection**: GDPR compliant

## 7. Pay-TV, OTT, and Advertising Market KPIs

### Pay-TV
- **Pay-TV ARPU**: ~€15-25/month
- **Pay-TV revenue growth rate**: ~4-5% annually
- **Number of major operators**: 3-4 (DigitAlb, Tring TV, Artmotion, SuperSport Albania)
- **Churn rate**: ~18-20% annually

### OTT/SVOD
- **Market size**: ~€50 million
- **Revenue/ARPU**: ~€6-8/month per service
- **Top players**: Netflix, DigitAlb Online, Tring TV Online
- **Subscriber growth rates**: ~15-18% annually

### Advertising
- **TV advertising revenue**: ~€80 million
- **AVOD/CTV ad spend**: ~€15 million
- **Growth forecasts**: ~5-6% annually
- **Programmatic adoption**: ~25%

## 8. Market Forecasts

- **Pay-TV household forecast (5-10 years)**: Growth (~3% annually)
- **OTT/SVOD penetration forecast**: Growth to ~45% by 2030
- **Broadband and FTTH growth**: FTTH to reach ~60% by 2030
- **Economic forecast**: Strong growth (~3.5% GDP annually)
- **Population forecast**: Stable to slight decline (~2.7 million by 2030)
