# Estonia - Demographics & Market Data

## 1. Core Population & Household Structure

### Population Basics
- **Total population**: ~1.37 million (Jan 2025), decreased by 5,402 from 2024
- **Urban vs rural**: ~70% urban, ~30% rural
- **Population density**: ~30 people/km²
- **Household count**: ~600,000 households
- **Average household size**: ~2.3 people

### Age Structure
- **0-14**: ~15%
- **15-24**: ~11%
- **25-44**: ~26%
- **45-64**: ~28%
- **65+**: ~20%
- **Median age**: ~43 years

### Household Types
- **Single-person households**: ~38%
- **Family households with children**: ~30%
- **Multi-generational homes**: ~5%
- **Socioeconomic segmentation**: ABC1 ~45%, C2DE ~55%

## 2. Economic Indicators

### Macro-economics
- **GDP (total)**: ~€35 billion (2024)
- **GDP per capita**: ~€25,000
- **GDP growth rate**: ~1.5% (2024)
- **Inflation rate**: ~4.0% (2024)
- **Disposable income per household**: ~€20,000/year
- **Cost of living index**: 70 (EU average = 100)

### Consumer Spend Capacity
- **Average telecom spend per household**: ~€40/month
- **Average entertainment/media spend**: ~€50/month
- **Credit card penetration**: ~70%
- **Digital payments adoption**: ~85%

## 3. Connectivity & Device Penetration

### Broadband
- **Broadband penetration**: ~93.7% of population are internet users (2024), ~92.9% of households have internet access
- **Average broadband speed**: ~150 Mbps
- **FTTH availability**: ~78.9% have gigabit-speed FTTP (2022), ~94.9% of households can access fixed broadband, government aims for full gigabit coverage by 2030
- **Mobile data penetration**: 4G/5G coverage ~98%
- **Mobile broadband ARPU**: ~€20/month

### Devices
- **Smart TV penetration**: ~65%
- **Streaming media players**: ~45%
- **Smartphone penetration**: ~92%
- **PC/tablet penetration**: ~88%
- **Console penetration**: ~38%

## 4. Television Access & Platform Penetration

### TV Platform Split
- **Total TV households**: ~600,000
- **Pay-TV households**: ~360,000 (~60%)
- **Free-to-air households**: ~240,000 (~40%)
- **Average daily TV viewing time**: ~221 minutes (2024), younger audiences (18-29) reduced viewing by 19%

### Market share by delivery type:
- **Satellite**: ~25%
- **Cable**: ~30%
- **IPTV/FTTH**: ~35%
- **DTT**: ~10%
- **OTT-only households**: ~12%

### Platform Technology & CA
- **CI+ penetration**: ~40% of pay-TV households
- **CAM vs STB share**: ~30% CAM, ~70% STB
- **Conditional Access systems**: Conax, Irdeto, Nagravision
- **Smartcard vs cardless CAS**: Mixed (cardless growing)
- **Operator-owned STBs vs retail devices**: ~70% operator STBs

### OTT & Streaming
- **SVOD penetration**: ~55% of households
- **AVOD penetration**: ~48% of households
- **Hybrid STB penetration**: ~45%
- **TV Everywhere usage**: ~35% of pay-TV subscribers

## 5. Language, Culture & Content Consumption

### Language
- **Primary language**: Estonian
- **% bilingual population**: ~70% (English as second language)
- **Requirements**: Estonian subtitling preferred

### Content Preferences
- **Top genres**: News, sports, movies, drama series, documentaries
- **Local content importance**: High - Estonian content preferred
- **Sports rights**: Football, Basketball, Skiing
- **Public service broadcaster influence**: Strong (ERR)

## 6. Regulatory Environment

### Broadcast Regulation
- **Licensing authorities**: TTJA (Consumer Protection and Technical Regulatory Authority)
- **Spectrum allocation**: DTT (MUX1-4), satellite (Eutelsat, SES)
- **Must-carry rules**: Yes - public channels must be carried
- **Local content quota**: European content quotas apply

### Consumer & Platform Regulation
- **Anti-piracy environment**: Moderate enforcement
- **CAS/DRM compliance**: Mandatory for pay-TV
- **CI+ certification requirements**: Yes, via CI+ Consortium
- **Taxes on TV subscriptions**: VAT 20%
- **Data protection**: GDPR compliant

## 7. Pay-TV, OTT, and Advertising Market KPIs

### Pay-TV
- **Pay-TV ARPU**: ~€25-35/month
- **Pay-TV revenue growth rate**: ~2-3% annually
- **Number of major operators**: 3-4 (Telia Estonia, Elisa Estonia, Starman)
- **Churn rate**: ~12-14% annually

### OTT/SVOD
- **Market size**: ~€80 million
- **Revenue/ARPU**: ~€8-10/month per service
- **Top players**: Netflix, HBO Max, Viaplay, ERR.ee
- **Subscriber growth rates**: ~10-12% annually

### Advertising
- **TV advertising revenue**: ~€100 million
- **AVOD/CTV ad spend**: ~€25 million
- **Growth forecasts**: ~3-4% annually
- **Programmatic adoption**: ~38%

## 8. Market Forecasts

- **Pay-TV household forecast (5-10 years)**: Stable to slight decline (~-1% annually)
- **OTT/SVOD penetration forecast**: Growth to ~70% by 2030
- **Broadband and FTTH growth**: Full gigabit coverage by 2030 (government target)
- **Economic forecast**: Moderate growth (~1.5% GDP annually)
- **Population forecast**: Decline to ~1.3 million by 2030 (9,646 births, 15,596 deaths in 2024, immigration slightly exceeding emigration)
