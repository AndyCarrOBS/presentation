# Cyprus - Demographics & Market Data

## 1. Core Population & Household Structure

### Population Basics
- **Total population**: ~1.27 million (2024)
- **Urban vs rural**: ~67% urban, ~33% rural
- **Population density**: ~137 people/km²
- **Household count**: ~350,000 households
- **Average household size**: ~3.6 people

### Age Structure
- **0-14**: ~15.4% (0-4: 5.1%, 5-12: 8.6%, 13-17: 5.1%)
- **15-24**: ~7.4% (18-24)
- **25-44**: ~32.8% (25-34: 14.8%, 35-44: 18.0%)
- **45-64**: ~25.6% (45-54: 13.9%, 55-64: 11.7%)
- **65+**: ~15.4%
- **Median age**: ~38 years

### Household Types
- **Single-person households**: ~22%
- **Family households with children**: ~48%
- **Multi-generational homes**: ~18%
- **Socioeconomic segmentation**: ABC1 ~45%, C2DE ~55%

## 2. Economic Indicators

### Macro-economics
- **GDP (total)**: ~€28 billion (2024)
- **GDP per capita**: ~€22,000
- **GDP growth rate**: ~2.5% (2024)
- **Inflation rate**: ~3.0% (2024)
- **Disposable income per household**: ~€20,000/year
- **Cost of living index**: 85 (EU average = 100)

### Consumer Spend Capacity
- **Average telecom spend per household**: ~€50/month
- **Average entertainment/media spend**: ~€60/month
- **Credit card penetration**: ~75%
- **Digital payments adoption**: ~70%

## 3. Connectivity & Device Penetration

### Broadband
- **Broadband penetration**: ~91.4% of population are internet users, ~99.88% of households have internet access
- **Average broadband speed**: ~120 Mbps
- **FTTH availability**: ~55% (growing)
- **Mobile data penetration**: 4G/5G coverage ~98%
- **Mobile broadband ARPU**: ~€25/month

### Devices
- **Smart TV penetration**: ~65%
- **Streaming media players**: ~45%
- **Smartphone penetration**: ~90%
- **PC/tablet penetration**: ~85%
- **Console penetration**: ~40%

## 4. Television Access & Platform Penetration

### TV Platform Split
- **Total TV households**: ~350,000
- **Pay-TV households**: ~245,000 (~70%)
- **Free-to-air households**: ~105,000 (~30%)

### Market share by delivery type:
- **Satellite**: ~35%
- **Cable**: ~30%
- **IPTV/FTTH**: ~25%
- **DTT**: ~10%
- **OTT-only households**: ~12%

### Platform Technology & CA
- **CI+ penetration**: ~45% of pay-TV households
- **CAM vs STB share**: ~35% CAM, ~65% STB
- **Conditional Access systems**: Nagravision, Irdeto, Viaccess
- **Smartcard vs cardless CAS**: Mixed (smartcard dominant)
- **Operator-owned STBs vs retail devices**: ~70% operator STBs

### OTT & Streaming
- **SVOD penetration**: ~55% of households
- **AVOD penetration**: ~48% of households
- **Hybrid STB penetration**: ~48%
- **TV Everywhere usage**: ~40% of pay-TV subscribers

## 5. Language, Culture & Content Consumption

### Language
- **Primary language**: Greek (south), Turkish (north)
- **% bilingual population**: ~60% (English as second language)
- **Requirements**: Content in Greek/Turkish depending on region

### Content Preferences
- **Top genres**: News, sports, movies, drama series, reality TV
- **Local content importance**: High - Cypriot content preferred
- **Sports rights**: Football, Basketball
- **Public service broadcaster influence**: Strong (CyBC)

## 6. Regulatory Environment

### Broadcast Regulation
- **Licensing authorities**: CRTA (Cyprus Radio Television Authority)
- **Spectrum allocation**: DTT, satellite (Eutelsat, SES)
- **Must-carry rules**: Yes - public channels must be carried
- **Local content quota**: European content quotas apply

### Consumer & Platform Regulation
- **Anti-piracy environment**: Moderate enforcement
- **CAS/DRM compliance**: Mandatory for pay-TV
- **CI+ certification requirements**: Yes, via CI+ Consortium
- **Taxes on TV subscriptions**: VAT 19%
- **Data protection**: GDPR compliant

## 7. Pay-TV, OTT, and Advertising Market KPIs

### Pay-TV
- **Pay-TV ARPU**: ~€35-45/month
- **Pay-TV revenue growth rate**: ~2-3% annually
- **Number of major operators**: 3-4 (CytaVision, PrimeTel, Cablenet)
- **Churn rate**: ~12-14% annually
