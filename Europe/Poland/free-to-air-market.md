# Poland Free-to-Air TV Market Analysis

## Executive Summary

Poland's free-to-air TV market is stable with traditional platforms (cable, satellite, DTT) maintaining significant shares while streaming services continue to grow. The market is characterized by strong public service broadcasters (TVP), major commercial broadcasters (TVN, Polsat), and a growing ecosystem of local OTT services that hero Polish content.

---

## 1. Free-to-Air Platform Distribution

### 1.1 Platform Breakdown (2025)

**Total TV Households: ~40 million (estimated)**

#### • Cable Reception (Free-to-Air Tier)
- **Households**: 33.7% of Polish households (2025)
- **Primary delivery method**: DOCSIS cable networks
- **Coverage**: Urban and suburban areas
- **Key characteristics**:
  - Basic cable tier includes free-to-air channels
  - Must-carry rules ensure TVP availability
  - Most popular delivery method

#### • Satellite Reception
- **Households**: 30.1% of TV households (2025)
- **Primary delivery method**: Satellite (Astra, Eutelsat)
- **Coverage**: Near universal
- **Key characteristics**: 
  - Free-to-air channels available without subscription
  - HD channels available
  - Popular delivery method

#### • Digital Terrestrial Television (DTT)
- **Households**: 23.5% of households (2025)
- **Primary delivery method**: DVB-T2 terrestrial network
- **Coverage**: Nationwide
- **Key characteristics**:
  - Free-to-air HD channels
  - No subscription required
  - Cost-effective alternative
  - Significant market share

#### • Internet-Based Reception (IPTV/OTT)
- **Households**: Growing trend
- **Primary delivery method**: IPTV, streaming apps, smart TV apps
- **Key characteristics**:
  - Streaming platforms capture 9.7% of total TV viewing time
  - Growing internet-based consumption

### 1.2 Platform Trends

**Key Developments:**
- **Cable**: Most popular (33.7% of households)
- **Satellite**: Second most popular (30.1% of households)
- **DTT**: Significant share (23.5% of households)
- **Streaming**: Growing (9.7% of viewing time)

## 2. Main Free-to-Air Broadcasters

### 2.1 Public Service Broadcasters

#### • **TVP (Telewizja Polska)**
- **Market Share**: 17.00% (September 2025)
- **Position**: Public service broadcaster
- **Main Channels**:
  - **TVP1** - Main national channel
  - **TVP2** - Entertainment and series
  - **TVP Info** - News channel
  - **TVP Sport** - Sports channel
  - **TVP Kultura** - Cultural channel
  - **TVP Historia** - History channel
- **Reach**: Near universal coverage (public service broadcaster)
- **Content Focus**: News, entertainment, sports, cultural content, regional content
- **Funding**: License fee + advertising
- **Key Characteristics**:
  - Strong news and information programming
  - Sports rights (gains from sports content)
  - Drama content (gains from drama content)
  - Regional content production
  - Must-carry on all platforms

### 2.2 Commercial Broadcasters

#### • **TVN (Warner Bros. Discovery)**
- **Market Share**: 22.4% total audience share (2024, viewers 4+), 7.98% commercial 20-54 demographic
- **Position**: #1 commercial broadcaster
- **Structure**: Owned by Warner Bros. Discovery
- **Main Channels**:
  - **TVN** - Main commercial channel
  - **TVN24** - News channel (6.0% total audience share)
  - **TVN7** - Entertainment
  - **TTV** - Entertainment
- **Reach**: Leading commercial broadcaster
- **Content Focus**: News, entertainment, series, reality TV
- **Key Characteristics**:
  - Market leader (22.4% total audience share)
  - Strong news programming (TVN24)
  - Entertainment and series
  - Local content production
- **OTT Service**: **Player.pl** - 22 FAST channels launched

#### • **Polsat Group**
- **Market Share**: 18.50% total video viewership (September 2025, viewers 4+)
- **Position**: #2 commercial broadcaster
- **Main Channels**:
  - **Polsat** - Main commercial channel
  - **Polsat 2** - Entertainment
  - **TV4** - Entertainment
  - **Polsat News** - News channel
- **Reach**: Major commercial broadcaster
- **Content Focus**: Entertainment, series, news
- **Key Characteristics**:
  - Strong entertainment programming
  - Local content production
  - Series acquisitions
- **OTT Service**: **Polsat Box Go** - Plans for 20 FAST channels

- **Local Content Preference**: Strong preference for Polish content

### 6.3 Regulatory Impact
- **Must-Carry Rules**: TVP must be carried on all platforms
- **License Fee**: Broadcasting fee funds public service broadcasters
- **Content Quotas**: European content quotas apply
