# TV Platform Operator: UPC Poland (P4)

## 1. Executive Summary

- **Overview of the operator's business**: UPC Poland, now operating as P4 following acquisition, is a major cable and telecommunications operator in Poland, offering TV, broadband internet, mobile, and fixed-line services. The company operates cable and IPTV services across Poland.

- **Key market position**: Major cable/telco operator in Poland, 16.4% revenue share in TV services market (2023), part of P4 Group

- **Geographic footprint**: Nationwide coverage in Poland

- **Ownership structure**: Part of P4 Group (following acquisition of UPC Polska)

- **Overall growth/decline assessment**: Transition - Following acquisition by P4, company entered TV services market with significant market share. Polish pay-TV market valued at PLN 6.9 billion in 2024 (+1.4% growth).
