# Poland - Demographics & Market Data

## 1. Core Population & Household Structure

### Population Basics
- **Total population**: ~37.97 million (2024)
- **Urban vs rural**: ~60% urban, ~40% rural
- **Population density**: ~122 people/km²
- **Household count**: ~15.5 million households
- **Average household size**: ~2.4 people

### Age Structure
- **0-14**: ~15%
- **15-24**: ~11%
- **25-44**: ~28%
- **45-64**: ~28%
- **65+**: ~18%
- **Median age**: ~42.5 years

### Household Types
- **Single-person households**: ~25%
- **Family households with children**: ~35%
- **Multi-generational homes**: ~12%
- **Socioeconomic segmentation**: ABC1 ~35%, C2DE ~65%

## 2. Economic Indicators

### Macro-economics
- **GDP (total)**: ~€700 billion (2024)
- **GDP per capita**: ~€18,500
- **GDP growth rate**: ~3.0% (2024)
- **Inflation rate**: ~4.5% (2024)
- **Disposable income per household**: ~€20,000/year
- **Cost of living index**: 55 (EU average = 100)

### Consumer Spend Capacity
- **Average telecom spend per household**: ~€35/month
- **Average entertainment/media spend**: ~€45/month
- **Credit card penetration**: ~55%
- **Digital payments adoption**: ~60%

## 3. Connectivity & Device Penetration

### Broadband
- **Broadband penetration**: 95.9% of households (2024)
- **Average broadband speed**: ~100 Mbps
- **FTTH availability**: ~45% (growing)
- **Mobile data penetration**: 4G/5G coverage ~95%
- **Mobile broadband ARPU**: ~€15/month

### Devices
- **Smart TV penetration**: ~55%
- **Streaming media players**: ~35%
- **Smartphone penetration**: ~80%
- **PC/tablet penetration**: ~70%
- **Console penetration**: ~30%

## 4. Television Access & Platform Penetration

### TV Platform Split
- **Total TV households**: ~15.5 million
- **Pay-TV households**: ~11.8 million (~76.3%)
- **Free-to-air households**: ~3.7 million (~23.7%)

### Market share by delivery type:
- **Satellite**: ~45%
- **Cable**: ~35%
- **IPTV/FTTH**: ~15%
- **DTT**: ~33% (free-to-air terrestrial)
- **OTT-only households**: ~10%

### Platform Technology & CA
- **CI+ penetration**: ~50% of pay-TV households
- **CAM vs STB share**: ~35% CAM, ~65% STB
- **Conditional Access systems**: Nagravision, Irdeto, Conax
- **Smartcard vs cardless CAS**: Smartcard dominant
- **Operator-owned STBs vs retail devices**: ~60% operator STBs

### OTT & Streaming
- **SVOD penetration**: ~45% of households
- **AVOD penetration**: ~40% of households
- **Hybrid STB penetration**: ~40%
- **TV Everywhere usage**: ~25% of pay-TV subscribers

## 5. Language, Culture & Content Consumption

### Language
- **Primary language**: Polish
- **% bilingual population**: ~30% (English as second language)
- **Requirements**: Dubbing preferred for movies/TV, subtitling for some content

### Content Preferences
- **Top genres**: News, sports, movies, drama series, reality TV
- **Local content importance**: High - Polish content preferred
- **Sports rights**: Football (Ekstraklasa), Volleyball, Handball
- **Public service broadcaster influence**: Strong (TVP)

## 6. Regulatory Environment

### Broadcast Regulation
- **Licensing authorities**: KRRiT (National Broadcasting Council)
- **Spectrum allocation**: DTT (MUX1-8), satellite (Eutelsat, SES)
- **Must-carry rules**: Yes - public channels must be carried
- **Local content quota**: European content quotas apply

### Consumer & Platform Regulation
- **Anti-piracy environment**: Moderate enforcement
- **CAS/DRM compliance**: Mandatory for pay-TV
- **CI+ certification requirements**: Yes, via CI+ Consortium
- **Taxes on TV subscriptions**: VAT 23%
- **Data protection**: GDPR compliant

## 7. Pay-TV, OTT, and Advertising Market KPIs

### Pay-TV
- **Pay-TV ARPU**: ~€25-35/month
- **Pay-TV revenue growth rate**: ~2-3% annually
- **Number of major operators**: 5-6 (Cyfrowy Polsat, Canal+, Vectra, Orange, UPC, Netia)
- **Churn rate**: ~15-18% annually

### OTT/SVOD
- **Market size**: ~€800 million
- **Revenue/ARPU**: ~€7-10/month per service
- **Top players**: Netflix, Canal+ Online, Player.pl, HBO Max, Disney+
- **Subscriber growth rates**: ~10-12% annually

### Advertising
- **TV advertising revenue**: ~€1.2 billion
- **AVOD/CTV ad spend**: ~€200 million
- **Growth forecasts**: ~3-4% annually
- **Programmatic adoption**: ~35%

## 8. Market Forecasts

- **Pay-TV household forecast (5-10 years)**: Stable to slight growth (~1% annually)
- **OTT/SVOD penetration forecast**: Growth to ~60% by 2030
- **Broadband and FTTH growth**: FTTH to reach ~70% by 2030
- **Economic forecast**: Moderate growth (~2.5% GDP annually)
- **Population forecast**: Decline to ~36 million by 2030

## 9. Competitive Landscape

### Major Operators

**Cyfrowy Polsat S.A. (Polsat Box)**
- Platform type: Satellite, OTT
- Subscriber numbers: ~4.843 million (end 2023)
- Market share: ~25.5% pay-TV
- Technology: CI+, Nagravision CAS, hybrid STBs
- Features: 4K, HDR, catch-up TV, OTT apps

**CANAL+ Polska S.A. (Platforma Canal+)**
- Platform type: Satellite, OTT
- Subscriber numbers: ~2.7 million (2019)
- Market share: ~17.3% pay-TV
- Technology: CI+ CAM modules, Nagravision CAS
- Features: 4K, premium sports, local content

**Vectra**
- Platform type: Cable
- Subscriber numbers: 711,000
- Market share: ~11.2% pay-TV, 15.8% cable TV
- Technology: CI+, operator STBs
- Features: 4K, triple-play bundles

**Orange Polska**
- Platform type: IPTV, OTT
- Subscriber numbers: 940,000 IPTV (end 2024)
- Market share: ~8.9% pay-TV
- Technology: Operator STBs, OTT apps
- Features: 4K, multi-device streaming

**UPC Polska (acquired by Play)**
- Platform type: Cable, IPTV
- Subscriber numbers: ~13.7% market share (2022)

- Features: 4K, triple-play bundles

**Netia**
- Platform type: IPTV
- Subscriber numbers: ~3.8% market share
- Market share: ~3.8% pay-TV
- Technology: IPTV (Netia Player), CI+ not typically supported
