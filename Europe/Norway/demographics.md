# Norway - Demographics & Market Data

## 1. Core Population & Household Structure

### Population Basics
- **Total population**: ~5.58 million (2024)
- **Urban vs rural**: ~83% urban, ~17% rural
- **Population density**: ~14 people/km²
- **Household count**: ~2.62 million private households (2024)
- **Average household size**: ~2.1 people

### Age Structure
- **0-14**: ~17%
- **15-24**: ~12%
- **25-44**: ~26%
- **45-64**: ~27%
- **65+**: ~18%
- **Median age**: ~39.7 years

### Household Types
- **Single-person households**: ~41% (1,067,725 individuals living alone)
- **Couples without children**: ~24% (636,291 households)
- **Married couples with children**: ~11% (300,651 households)
- **Cohabiting couples with children**: ~7% (187,170 households)
- **Socioeconomic segmentation**: ABC1 ~55%, C2DE ~45%

## 2. Economic Indicators

### Macro-economics
- **GDP (total)**: ~€450 billion (2024)
- **GDP per capita**: ~€81,000
- **GDP growth rate**: ~1.5% (2024)
- **Inflation rate**: ~3.0% (2024)
- **Disposable income per household**: ~€55,000/year
- **Cost of living index**: 150 (EU average = 100)

### Consumer Spend Capacity
- **Average telecom spend per household**: ~€70/month
- **Average entertainment/media spend**: ~€100/month
- **Credit card penetration**: ~95%
- **Digital payments adoption**: ~90%

## 3. Connectivity & Device Penetration

### Broadband
- **Broadband penetration**: ~99% of households
- **Average broadband speed**: ~200 Mbps
- **FTTH availability**: ~85% (growing)
- **Mobile data penetration**: 4G/5G coverage ~99%
- **Mobile broadband ARPU**: ~€35/month
- **99.1% of households have access to ≥100 Mbps download speed** (end 2024), **96.2% have access to ≥1000 Mbps** (end 2024)

### Devices
- **Smart TV penetration**: ~78%
- **Streaming media players**: ~60%
- **Smartphone penetration**: ~95%
- **PC/tablet penetration**: ~92%
- **Console penetration**: ~50%

## 4. Television Access & Platform Penetration

### TV Platform Split
- **Total TV households**: ~2.62 million
- **Pay-TV households**: ~2.1 million (~80%)
- **Free-to-air households**: ~520,000 (~20%)

### Market share by delivery type:
- **Satellite**: ~30%
- **Cable**: ~25%
- **IPTV/FTTH**: ~40%
- **DTT**: ~5%
- **OTT-only households**: ~20%

### Platform Technology & CA
- **CI+ penetration**: ~50% of pay-TV households
- **CAM vs STB share**: ~35% CAM, ~65% STB
- **Conditional Access systems**: Conax, Irdeto, Nagravision
- **Smartcard vs cardless CAS**: Mixed (cardless growing)
- **Operator-owned STBs vs retail devices**: ~70% operator STBs

### OTT & Streaming
- **SVOD penetration**: ~80% of households subscribe to at least one paid streaming service (May 2025, +6% from previous year)
- **AVOD penetration**: ~65% of households
- **Hybrid STB penetration**: ~60%
- **TV Everywhere usage**: ~55% of pay-TV subscribers

## 5. Language, Culture & Content Consumption

### Language
- **Primary language**: Norwegian
- **% bilingual population**: ~90% (English as second language)
- **Requirements**: Subtitling preferred over dubbing

### Content Preferences
- **Top genres**: News, sports, movies, drama series, crime dramas, documentaries
- **Local content importance**: High - Norwegian content preferred
- **Sports rights**: Football, Skiing, Biathlon, Handball
- **Public service broadcaster influence**: Very strong (NRK)

## 6. Regulatory Environment

### Broadcast Regulation
- **Licensing authorities**: NMA (Norwegian Media Authority)
- **Spectrum allocation**: DTT (MUX1-5), satellite (Thor, Intelsat)
- **Must-carry rules**: Yes - public channels must be carried
- **Local content quota**: European content quotas apply

### Consumer & Platform Regulation
- **Anti-piracy environment**: Strong enforcement
- **CAS/DRM compliance**: Mandatory for pay-TV
- **CI+ certification requirements**: Yes, via CI+ Consortium
- **Taxes on TV subscriptions**: VAT 25%
- **Data protection**: GDPR compliant

## 7. Pay-TV, OTT, and Advertising Market KPIs

### Pay-TV
- **Pay-TV ARPU**: ~€50-60/month
- **Pay-TV revenue growth rate**: ~1-2% annually
- **Number of major operators**: 3-4 (Telenor, Telia Norway, Allente, Altibox)
- **Churn rate**: ~10-12% annually

### OTT/SVOD
- **Market size**: ~€600 million
- **Revenue/ARPU**: ~€12-15/month per service
- **Top players**: Netflix, Viaplay, HBO Max, Disney+, TV 2 Play
- **Subscriber growth rates**: ~8-10% annually

### Advertising
- **TV advertising revenue**: ~€700 million
- **AVOD/CTV ad spend**: ~€180 million
- **Growth forecasts**: ~2-3% annually
- **Programmatic adoption**: ~52%

## 8. Market Forecasts

- **Pay-TV household forecast (5-10 years)**: Stable to slight decline (~-1% annually)
- **OTT/SVOD penetration forecast**: Growth to ~88% by 2030
- **Broadband and FTTH growth**: Gigabit coverage to reach ~99% by 2030
- **Economic forecast**: Moderate growth (~1.5% GDP annually)
- **Population forecast**: Growth to ~5.8 million by 2030

## 9. Competitive Landscape

### Major Operators

**Telenor Norway**
- Platform type: Cable, IPTV, Satellite
- Subscriber numbers: Largest pay-TV operator
- Market share: ~35% pay-TV
- Technology: CI+ CAM modules, operator STBs
- Features: 4K, Telenor TV, multi-device

**Telia Norway**
- Platform type: Cable, IPTV
- Subscriber numbers: Growing
- Market share: ~25% pay-TV
- Technology: CI+ CAM modules
- Features: 4K, Telia TV

**Allente**
- Platform type: Satellite, IPTV
- Subscriber numbers: Growing (formed from Canal Digital and Viasat Consumer merger)
- Market share: ~20% pay-TV
- Technology: Smartcards, CI+ compatibility varies
- Features: Multi-platform offering

**Altibox**
- Platform type: Cable, IPTV
- Subscriber numbers: Growing
- Market share: ~12% pay-TV
- Technology: CI+ CAM modules
- Features: 4K, triple-play bundles
