- **Average household size**: ~2.7 people

- **0-14**: ~18.5% (0-4: 5.0%, 5-12: 8.0%, 13-17: 5.5%)
- **15-24**: ~7.8% (18-24)
- **25-44**: ~31.5% (25-34: 15.0%, 35-44: 16.5%)
- **45-64**: ~26.5% (45-54: 14.0%, 55-64: 12.5%)

## 5. Language, Culture & Content Consumption

### Language
- **Primary language**: Icelandic
- **% bilingual population**: ~95% (English as second language)
- **Requirements**: Subtitling preferred over dubbing

### Content Preferences
- **Top genres**: News, sports, movies, drama series, documentaries
- **Local content importance**: High - Icelandic content preferred
- **Sports rights**: Football, Handball
- **Public service broadcaster influence**: Very strong (RÚV)

## 6. Regulatory Environment

### Broadcast Regulation
- **Licensing authorities**: IMC (Icelandic Media Commission)
- **Spectrum allocation**: DTT, satellite (Thor, Intelsat)
- **Must-carry rules**: Yes - public channels must be carried
- **Local content quota**: European content quotas apply

### Consumer & Platform Regulation
- **Anti-piracy environment**: Strong enforcement
- **CAS/DRM compliance**: Mandatory for pay-TV
- **CI+ certification requirements**: Yes, via CI+ Consortium
- **Taxes on TV subscriptions**: VAT 24%
- **Data protection**: GDPR compliant
