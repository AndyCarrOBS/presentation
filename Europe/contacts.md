# Broadcast & OTT Engagement Contacts
## Organizations to Engage for Smart TV Market Entry: Germany & Neighboring Countries

---

## Executive Summary

This document provides a comprehensive list of organizations that need to be engaged with from a broadcast and OTT perspective to position a new TV platform in Germany and its neighboring countries. Each entry includes organization name, location of broadcast operations, group/department to engage with, and recommended contact roles.

**Engagement Priority:**
- **Priority 1**: Public service broadcasters (must-carry rules, market leaders)
- **Priority 2**: Commercial broadcasters (significant market share, OTT platforms)
- **Priority 3**: Regulatory bodies (licensing, compliance)
- **Priority 4**: Industry associations (standards, best practices)
- **Priority 5**: Technology partners (HbbTV, infrastructure)

## 13. Appendix

### Engagement Best Practices

**Initial Contact:**
- Research organization structure and key personnel
- Identify specific group/department relevant to your needs
- Prepare value proposition aligned with broadcaster goals
- Respect content rights and regulatory requirements

**Partnership Approach:**
- Emphasize public service mission alignment (for PSBs)
- Offer value exchange (audience reach, analytics, technical support)
- Ensure compliance with must-carry rules and regulations
- Respect broadcaster branding and content rights

**Technical Integration:**
- Ensure HbbTV compliance for broadcast-triggered applications
- Plan for proprietary app porting (multiple platforms)
- Factor in certification and testing timelines
- Consider regulatory mandates (Belgium VRT Max pre-installation)

### Data Sources
- Free-to-air market analyses
- OTT platform analysis
- Web research on organizational structures
- Industry reports and press releases

- Analysis of market research documents
