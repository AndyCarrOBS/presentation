# TV Platform Operator: Allente

## 1. Executive Summary

- **Overview of the operator's business**: Allente is a Nordic TV distributor operating satellite pay-TV services across Denmark, Finland, Norway, and Sweden. The platform provides premium content including sports, movies, and entertainment channels via satellite delivery.

- **Key market position**: Major Nordic satellite pay-TV platform, ~840,000 subscribers across Nordic countries (Denmark, Finland, Norway, Sweden)

- **Geographic footprint**: Denmark, Finland, Norway, Sweden

- **Ownership structure**: Acquired by Viaplay Group in July 2025 (Viaplay acquired remaining 50% stake from Telenor for SEK 1.1 billion, making Viaplay sole owner)

- **Overall growth/decline assessment**: Stable - Revenue stable around SEK 6.5-6.8 billion, strong EBITDA margins. Viaplay acquisition positions for integrated streaming and satellite strategy.
