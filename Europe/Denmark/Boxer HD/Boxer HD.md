# TV Platform Operator: Boxer HD

## 1. Executive Summary

- **Overview of the operator's business**: Boxer HD (Boxer TV Access) is a terrestrial pay-TV platform in Denmark, providing pay television channels on Denmark's digital terrestrial network. Owned by Tele2 AB, Boxer HD offers premium content via DTT (Digital Terrestrial Television).

- **Key market position**: Terrestrial pay-TV platform in Denmark, owned by Tele2 AB

- **Geographic footprint**: Denmark (terrestrial coverage)

- **Ownership structure**: Subsidiary of Tele2 AB (Swedish telecommunications company)

- **Overall growth/decline assessment**: Transition - Migration from legacy DTT platform to modern cable and IP-based services completed by January 1, 2025. Terrestrial revenue ceased from Q1 2025, with revenue expected to decline by ~SEK 200 million in 2025, but EBITDA impact neutral.

## 4. Growth Assessment

### 4.1 Market Position

- Ranking vs. competitors: Terrestrial pay-TV platform, smaller market presence
- Strength in broadband, mobile, and TV: Focus on TV services via terrestrial delivery (now migrating)
- Differentiators: Terrestrial delivery provided universal coverage, now transitioning to modern platforms

### 4.2 Growth Indicators

- Subscriber trends across FTTH, broadband, TV, and mobile: Migration to cable/IP platforms
- ARPU trends: Data not publicly disclosed
- Network upgrades: Migration from DTT to cable/IP completed January 1, 2025
- New service launches: Cable and IP-based services

### 4.3 Decline Indicators

- Cord-cutting pressure: Moderate pressure from OTT competitors
- Regulatory or pricing pressure: DTT platform migration reflects market evolution
- Content rights losses: Standard content portfolio maintained
- Competitive erosion from OTT: Competition from Netflix, Disney+, Amazon Prime Video

### **Overall Assessment**

- **Operator Status:** Transition

- **Drivers Behind the Trend:** Migration from legacy digital terrestrial television (DTT) platform to modern cable and IP-based services completed by January 1, 2025. This strategic transition reflects market evolution toward modern delivery platforms. Terrestrial revenue ceased from Q1 2025, with revenue expected to decline by ~SEK 200 million in 2025 compared to 2024. However, EBITDA impact is projected to be neutral, indicating cost savings offset revenue decline. Tele2 Group reported that total end-user service revenue declined by 1% in Q1 2025, attributed to Boxer TV migration and continued decline in other legacy services. Migration positions Boxer HD for future competitiveness on modern platforms.
