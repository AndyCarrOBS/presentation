# Bulgaria - Demographics & Market Data

## 1. Core Population & Household Structure

### Population Basics
- **Total population**: ~6.44 million (Dec 2024), decreased by 8,121 from 2023 (-0.13%)
- **Urban vs rural**: ~77.5% urban (projected by 2029), ~22.5% rural
- **Population density**: ~58 people/km²
- **Household count**: ~2.5 million households
- **Average household size**: ~2.6 people

### Age Structure
- **0-14**: ~14%
- **15-24**: ~11%
- **25-44**: ~26%
- **45-64**: ~28%
- **65+**: ~24%
- **Median age**: ~45.3 years

### Household Types
- **Single-person households**: ~37.2% (2024), projected to reach 38.9% by 2029
- **Family households with children**: ~35%
- **Multi-generational homes**: ~15%
- **Socioeconomic segmentation**: ABC1 ~30%, C2DE ~70%

## 2. Economic Indicators

### Macro-economics
- **GDP (total)**: ~€80 billion (2024)
- **GDP per capita**: ~€12,500
- **GDP growth rate**: ~2.5% (2024)
- **Inflation rate**: ~4.5% (2024)
- **Disposable income per household**: ~€15,000/year
- **Cost of living index**: 45 (EU average = 100)

### Consumer Spend Capacity
- **Average telecom spend per household**: ~€30/month
- **Average entertainment/media spend**: ~€35/month
- **Credit card penetration**: ~45%
- **Digital payments adoption**: ~50%

## 3. Connectivity & Device Penetration

### Broadband
- **Broadband penetration**: ~92.1% of households (2024), +3.6pp from previous year
- **Average broadband speed**: ~100 Mbps
- **FTTH availability**: ~50% (growing)
- **Mobile data penetration**: 4G/5G coverage ~95%
- **Mobile broadband ARPU**: ~€12/month
- **Urban households**: ~94.7% internet penetration, rural: ~84.3% (urban-rural divide narrowed from 29.6 to 10.4pp over past decade)

### Devices
- **Smart TV penetration**: ~48%
- **Streaming media players**: ~30%
- **Smartphone penetration**: ~78%
- **PC/tablet penetration**: ~68%
- **Console penetration**: ~25%

## 4. Television Access & Platform Penetration

### TV Platform Split
- **Total TV households**: ~2.5 million
- **Pay-TV households**: ~1.76 million (~70.4% of households, Dec 2024, +0.3pp from previous year)
- **Free-to-air households**: ~740,000 (~29.6%)

### Market share by delivery type:
- **Satellite**: ~35%
- **Cable**: ~40%
- **IPTV/FTTH**: ~33.6% penetration among households (2024, +2.9pp from 2023)
- **DTT**: ~5%
- **OTT-only households**: ~8%

### Platform Technology & CA
- **CI+ penetration**: ~40% of pay-TV households
- **CAM vs STB share**: ~30% CAM, ~70% STB
- **Conditional Access systems**: Nagravision, Irdeto, Conax
- **Smartcard vs cardless CAS: Mixed (smartcard dominant)
- **Operator-owned STBs**: ~65% operator STBs

### OTT & Streaming
- **SVOD penetration**: ~35% of households
- **AVOD penetration**: ~32%
- **Hybrid STB**: ~35%
- **TV Everywhere**: ~22% of pay-TV subscribers

## 5. Language, Culture & Content Consumption

### Language
- **Primary language**: Bulgarian
- **% bilingual population**: ~25% (English as second language)
- **Requirements**: Bulgarian dubbing/subtitling preferred

### Content Preferences
- **Top genres**: News, sports, movies, drama series, reality TV
- **Local content importance**: High - Bulgarian content preferred
- **Sports rights**: Football, Handball, Tennis
- **Public service broadcaster influence**: Strong (BNT)

## 6. Regulatory Environment

### Broadcast Regulation
- **Licensing authorities**: CRC (Communications Regulation Commission)
- **Spectrum allocation**: DTT (MUX1-8), satellite (Eutelsat, SES)
- **Must-carry rules**: Yes - public channels must be carried
- **Local content quota**: European content quotas apply

### Consumer & Platform Regulation
- **Anti-piracy environment**: Moderate enforcement
- **CAS/DRM compliance**: Mandatory for pay-TV
- **CI+ certification requirements**: Yes, via CI+ Consortium
- **Taxes on TV subscriptions**: VAT 20%
- **Data protection**: GDPR compliant

## 7. Pay-TV, OTT, and Advertising Market KPIs

### Pay-TV
- **Pay-TV ARPU**: ~€20-28/month
- **Pay-TV revenue growth**: ~2-3% annually
- **Number of major operators**: 4-5 (Bulsatcom, Vivacom, A1 Bulgaria, Nova Broadcasting Group)
- **Churn rate**: ~16-18% annually

### OTT/SVOD
- **Market size**: ~€150 million
- **Revenue/ARPU**: ~€7-9/month per service
- **Top players**: Netflix, HBO Max, Voyo, O2 TV Slovakia

### Advertising
- **TV advertising revenue**: ~€250 million
- **AVOD/CTV ad spend**: ~€50 million
- **Growth forecasts**: ~3-4% annually
- **Programmatic adoption**: ~30%

## 8. Market Forecasts

- **Pay-TV household forecast (5-10 years)**: Stable to slight growth (~1% annually)
- **OTT/SVOD penetration forecast**: Growth to ~50% by 2030
- **Broadband and FTTH growth**: FTTP to reach ~80% by 2030
- **Economic forecast**: Moderate growth (~2.5% GDP annually)
- **Population forecast**: Decline to ~6.2 million by 2030

## 9. Competitive Landscape

### Major Operators

**Bulsatcom**
- Platform type: Satellite, Cable
- Subscriber numbers: Largest pay-TV operator
- Market share: ~25% pay-TV
- Technology: CI+ CAM modules
- Features: 4K, multi-channel packages

**Vivacom**
- Platform type: Cable, IPTV
- Subscriber numbers: Growing
- Market share: ~20% pay-TV
- Technology: CI+ CAM modules
- Features: 4K, triple-play bundles

**A1 Bulgaria**
- Platform type: IPTV
- Subscriber numbers: Growing
- Market share: ~15% pay-TV
- Technology: Operator STBs
- Features: 4K, multi-device streaming

**Nova Broadcasting Group**
- Platform type: Commercial broadcaster
- Subscriber numbers: Major commercial broadcaster
- Market share: ~30% TV market share
- Technology: Free-to-air + premium services
- Features: Nova TV, Nova Sport
