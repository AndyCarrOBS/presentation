# Azerbaijan - Demographics & Market Data

## 1. Core Population & Household Structure

### Population Basics
- **Total population**: ~10.2 million (2024 World Population Data Sheet)
- **Urban vs rural**: ~56% urban, ~44% rural
- **Population density**: ~118 people/km²
- **Household count**: ~3.2 million households
- **Average household size**: ~3.2 people

### Age Structure
- **0-14**: ~22%
- **15-24**: ~16%
- **25-44**: ~32%
- **45-64**: ~22%
- **65+**: ~8%
- **Median age**: ~32 years

### Household Types
- **Single-person households**: ~12%
- **Family households with children**: ~60%
- **Multi-generational homes**: ~20%
- **Socioeconomic segmentation**: ABC1 ~35%, C2DE ~65%

## 2. Economic Indicators

### Macro-economics
- **GDP (total)**: ~€70 billion (2024)
- **GDP per capita**: ~€6,900
- **GDP growth rate**: ~4.0% (2024)
- **Inflation rate**: ~5.5% (2024)
- **Disposable income per household**: ~€9,000/year
- **Cost of living index**: 38 (EU average = 100)

### Consumer Spend Capacity
- **Average telecom spend per household**: ~€26/month
- **Average entertainment/media spend**: ~€33/month
- **Credit card penetration**: ~42%
- **Digital payments adoption**: ~52%

## 3. Connectivity & Device Penetration

### Broadband
- **Broadband penetration**: ~49.0% of population use internet (2021)
- **Average broadband speed**: ~75 Mbps
- **FTTH availability**: ~35% (growing)
- **Mobile data penetration**: 4G/5G coverage ~88%
- **Mobile broadband ARPU**: ~€11/month

### Devices
- **Smart TV penetration**: ~38%
- **Streaming media players**: ~16%
- **Smartphone penetration**: ~70%
- **PC/tablet penetration**: ~60%
- **Console penetration**: ~14%

## 4. Television Access & Platform Penetration

### TV Platform Split
- **Total TV households**: ~3.2 million
- **Pay-TV households**: ~1.9 million (~60%)
- **Free-to-air households**: ~1.3 million (~40%)

### Market share by delivery type:
- **Satellite**: ~35%
- **Cable**: ~30%
- **IPTV/FTTH**: ~25%
- **DTT**: ~10%
- **OTT-only households**: ~7%

### Platform Technology & CA
- **CI+ penetration**: ~30% of pay-TV households
- **CAM vs STB share**: ~25% CAM, ~75% STB
- **Conditional Access systems**: Nagravision, Irdeto, Viaccess
- **Smartcard vs cardless CAS**: Smartcard dominant
- **Operator-owned STBs vs retail devices**: ~75% operator STBs

### OTT & Streaming
- **SVOD penetration**: ~26% of households
- **AVOD penetration**: ~22% of households
- **Hybrid STB penetration**: ~20%
- **TV Everywhere usage**: ~15% of pay-TV subscribers

## 5. Language, Culture & Content Consumption

### Language
- **Primary language**: Azerbaijani
- **% bilingual population**: ~40% (Russian/Turkish as second language)
- **Requirements**: Azerbaijani dubbing/subtitling preferred

### Content Preferences
- **Top genres**: News, sports, movies, drama series, reality TV
- **Local content importance**: Very high - Azerbaijani content preferred
- **Sports rights**: Football, Wrestling, Chess
- **Public service broadcaster influence**: Strong (AzTV)

## 6. Regulatory Environment

### Broadcast Regulation
- **Licensing authorities**: National TV and Radio Council
- **Spectrum allocation**: DTT, satellite (Azerspace, Eutelsat)
- **Must-carry rules**: Yes - public channels must be carried
- **Local content quota**: Azerbaijani content quotas apply

### Consumer & Platform Regulation
- **Anti-piracy environment**: Moderate enforcement
- **CAS/DRM compliance**: Mandatory for pay-TV
- **CI+ certification requirements**: Yes, via CI+ Consortium
- **Taxes on TV subscriptions**: VAT 18%
- **Data protection**: Local data protection laws

## 7. Pay-TV, OTT, and Advertising Market KPIs

### Pay-TV
