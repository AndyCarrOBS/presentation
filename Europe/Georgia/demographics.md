# Georgia - Demographics & Market Data

## 1. Core Population & Household Structure

### Population Basics
- **Total population**: ~3.7 million (2024 World Population Data Sheet)
- **Urban vs rural**: ~59% urban, ~41% rural
- **Population density**: ~53 people/km²
- **Household count**: ~1.2 million households
- **Average household size**: ~3.1 people

### Age Structure
- **0-14**: ~18%
- **15-24**: ~14%
- **25-44**: ~30%
- **45-64**: ~24%
- **65+**: ~14%
- **Median age**: ~38 years

### Household Types
- **Single-person households**: ~20%
- **Family households with children**: ~52%
- **Multi-generational homes**: ~18%
- **Socioeconomic segmentation**: ABC1 ~30%, C2DE ~70%

## 2. Economic Indicators

### Macro-economics
- **GDP (total)**: ~€22 billion (2024)
- **GDP per capita**: ~€5,900
- **GDP growth rate**: ~5.0% (2024)
- **Inflation rate**: ~3.5% (2024)
- **Disposable income per household**: ~€8,500/year
- **Cost of living index**: 35 (EU average = 100)

### Consumer Spend Capacity
- **Average telecom spend per household**: ~€25/month
- **Average entertainment/media spend**: ~€32/month
- **Credit card penetration**: ~40%
- **Digital payments adoption**: ~50%

## 3. Connectivity & Device Penetration

### Broadband
- **Broadband penetration**: ~54.1% of population use internet (2021)
- **Average broadband speed**: ~85 Mbps
- **FTTH availability**: ~35% (growing)
- **Mobile data penetration**: 4G/5G coverage ~92%
- **Mobile broadband ARPU**: ~€11/month
- **MagtiCom**: Largest mobile operator (41.2% market share), ~363,507 IPTV subscribers (June 2025), 99% population coverage

### Devices
- **Smart TV penetration**: ~42%
- **Streaming media players**: ~20%
- **Smartphone penetration**: ~75%
- **PC/tablet penetration**: ~65%
- **Console penetration**: ~16%

## 4. Television Access & Platform Penetration

### TV Platform Split
- **Total TV households**: ~1.2 million
- **Pay-TV households**: ~720,000 (~60%)
- **Free-to-air households**: ~480,000 (~40%)

### Market share by delivery type:
- **Satellite**: ~30%
- **Cable**: ~25%
- **IPTV/FTTH**: ~30%
- **DTT**: ~15%
- **OTT-only households**: ~8%

### Platform Technology & CA
- **CI+ penetration**: ~30% of pay-TV households
- **CAM vs STB share**: ~25% CAM, ~75% STB
- **Conditional Access systems**: Nagravision, Irdeto, Conax
- **Smartcard vs cardless CAS**: Smartcard dominant
- **Operator-owned STBs vs retail devices**: ~70% operator STBs

### OTT & Streaming
- **SVOD penetration**: ~30% of households
- **AVOD penetration**: ~26% of households
- **Hybrid STB penetration**: ~24%
- **TV Everywhere usage**: ~18% of pay-TV subscribers

## 5. Language, Culture & Content Consumption

### Language
- **Primary language**: Georgian
- **% bilingual population**: ~40% (English/Russian as second language)
- **Requirements**: Georgian dubbing/subtitling preferred

### Content Preferences
- **Top genres**: News, sports, movies, drama series, reality TV
- **Local content importance**: Very high - Georgian content preferred
- **Sports rights**: Football, Basketball, Rugby
- **Public service broadcaster influence**: Strong (GPB - Georgian Public Broadcasting)

## 6. Regulatory Environment

### Broadcast Regulation
- **Licensing authorities**: GNCC (Georgian National Communications Commission)
- **Spectrum allocation**: DTT, satellite (Eutelsat, SES)
- **Must-carry rules**: Yes - public channels must be carried
- **Local content quota**: Georgian content quotas apply

### Consumer & Platform Regulation
- **Anti-piracy environment**: Moderate enforcement
- **CAS/DRM compliance**: Mandatory for pay-TV
- **CI+ certification requirements**: Yes, via CI+ Consortium
- **Taxes on TV subscriptions**: VAT 18%
- **Data protection**: Local data protection laws

## 7. Pay-TV, OTT, and Advertising Market KPIs

### Pay-TV
- **Pay-TV ARPU**: ~€16-24/month
- **Pay-TV revenue growth rate**: ~5-6% annually
- **Number of major operators**: 3-4 (MagtiCom, Silknet, MagtiSat, Cable Operators)
- **Churn rate**: ~18-20% annually

### OTT/SVOD
- **Market size**: ~€60 million
- **Revenue/ARPU**: ~€6-8/month per service
- **Top players**: Netflix, HBO Max, MagtiCom TV Online
- **Subscriber growth rates**: ~18-20% annually

### Advertising
- **TV advertising revenue**: ~€80 million
- **AVOD/CTV ad spend**: ~€15 million
- **Growth forecasts**: ~6-7% annually
- **Programmatic adoption**: ~26%

## 8. Market Forecasts

- **Pay-TV household forecast (5-10 years)**: Growth (~3% annually)
- **OTT/SVOD penetration forecast**: Growth to ~48% by 2030
- **Broadband and FTTH growth**: FTTH to reach ~55% by 2030
- **Economic forecast**: Strong growth (~5.0% GDP annually)
- **Population forecast**: Stable to slight decline (~3.6 million by 2030)

## 9. Competitive Landscape

### Major Operators

**MagtiCom**
- Platform type: IPTV
- Subscriber numbers: ~363,507 IPTV (June 2025), ~2.35 million mobile (41.2% market share)
- Market share: ~30% pay-TV
- Technology: Operator STBs, MagtiCom TV platform
- Features: 4K, multi-device streaming, 99% population coverage

**Silknet**
- Platform type: IPTV, Cable
- Subscriber numbers: Growing
- Market share: ~25% pay-TV
- Technology: Operator STBs, CI+ CAM modules
- Features: Triple-play bundles

**MagtiSat**
- Platform type: Satellite
- Subscriber numbers: Growing (first digital satellite pay-TV service, 2012)
- Market share: ~20% pay-TV
- Technology: Smartcards, CI+ compatibility varies
- Features: Standard and HD channels

**Cable Operators**
- Platform type: Cable
- Subscriber numbers: Unified by Electronic Communications Association of Georgia
- Market share: ~15% pay-TV
- Technology: CI+ CAM modules
- Features: Local cable networks
