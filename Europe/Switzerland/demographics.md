# Switzerland - Demographics & Market Data

## 1. Core Population & Household Structure

### Population Basics
- **Total population**: ~9.03 million (2024)
- **Urban vs rural**: ~74% urban, ~26% rural
- **Population density**: ~219 people/km²
- **Household count**: ~4.25 million private households
- **Average household size**: ~2.1 people

### Age Structure
- **0-14**: ~15%
- **15-24**: ~12%
- **25-44**: ~26%
- **45-64**: ~27%
- **65+**: ~20%
- **Median age**: ~43 years

### Household Types
- **Single-person households**: ~38%
- **Family households with children**: ~28%
- **Multi-generational homes**: ~6%
- **Socioeconomic segmentation**: ABC1 ~55%, C2DE ~45%

## 2. Economic Indicators

### Macro-economics
- **GDP (total)**: ~€800 billion (2024)
- **GDP per capita**: ~€89,000
- **GDP growth rate**: ~1.2% (2024)
- **Inflation rate**: ~1.5% (2024)
- **Disposable income per household**: ~€65,000/year
- **Cost of living index**: 150 (EU average = 100)

### Consumer Spend Capacity
- **Average telecom spend per household**: ~€75/month
- **Average entertainment/media spend**: ~€110/month
- **Credit card penetration**: ~95%
- **Digital payments adoption**: ~90%

## 3. Connectivity & Device Penetration

### Broadband
- **Broadband penetration**: ~46.75% of population have broadband internet connection (mid-2024, top 3 OECD countries)
- **Average broadband speed**: ~180 Mbps
- **FTTH availability**: ~32% of total broadband connections are fiber-optic (FTTH/B, end 2024), ~1.4 million connections, +16.3% growth from June 2023
- **Mobile data penetration**: 4G LTE coverage ~99% of population, 5G coverage ~99% (2024)
- **Mobile broadband ARPU**: ~€35/month

### Devices
- **Smart TV penetration**: ~70%
- **Streaming media players**: ~55%
- **Smartphone penetration**: ~94%
- **PC/tablet penetration**: ~90%
- **Console penetration**: ~46%

## 4. Television Access & Platform Penetration

### TV Platform Split
- **Total TV households**: ~4.25 million
- **Pay-TV households**: ~2.5 million (~60%)
- **Free-to-air households**: ~1.75 million (~40%)
- **91% of private households have at least one traditional TV set** (2024), average 1.4 sets per TV household, over 5 million TV sets nationwide

### Market share by delivery type:
- **Satellite**: ~25%
- **Cable**: ~40% (declining, less than 40% by 2024)
- **IPTV/FTTH**: ~60% (predominant, over 60% of TV households by 2024)
- **DTT**: ~5%
- **OTT-only households**: ~12%

### Platform Technology & CA
- **CI+ penetration**: ~50% of pay-TV households
- **CAM vs STB share**: ~35% CAM, ~65% STB
- **Conditional Access systems**: Nagravision, Irdeto, Conax
- **Smartcard vs cardless CAS**: Mixed (smartcard dominant)
- **Operator-owned STBs vs retail devices**: ~70% operator STBs

### OTT & Streaming
- **SVOD penetration**: ~65% of households
- **AVOD penetration**: ~55% of households
- **Hybrid STB penetration**: ~55%
- **TV Everywhere usage**: ~48% of pay-TV subscribers

## 5. Language, Culture & Content Consumption

### Language
- **Primary language**: German (63%), French (23%), Italian (8%), Romansh (1%)
- **% bilingual population**: ~65% (multilingual country)
- **Requirements**: Content in German/French/Italian depending on region

### Content Preferences
- **Top genres**: News, sports, movies, drama series, documentaries
- **Local content importance**: High - Swiss content preferred
- **Sports rights**: Football, Ice Hockey, Skiing, Tennis
- **Public service broadcaster influence**: Strong (SRG SSR)

## 6. Regulatory Environment

### Broadcast Regulation
- **Licensing authorities**: BAKOM (Federal Office of Communications)
- **Spectrum allocation**: DTT, satellite (Astra, Eutelsat)
- **Must-carry rules**: Yes - public channels must be carried
- **Local content quota**: European content quotas apply

### Consumer & Platform Regulation
- **Anti-piracy environment**: Strong enforcement
- **CAS/DRM compliance**: Mandatory for pay-TV
- **CI+ certification requirements**: Yes, via CI+ Consortium
- **Taxes on TV subscriptions**: VAT 7.7%, TV licence fee (Billag)
- **Data protection**: GDPR compliant

## 7. Pay-TV, OTT, and Advertising Market KPIs

### Pay-TV
- **Pay-TV ARPU**: ~€55-65/month
- **Pay-TV revenue growth rate**: ~1-2% annually
- **Number of major operators**: 4-5 (Swisscom TV, UPC Switzerland, Sunrise, Salt, Zattoo)
- **Churn rate**: ~10-12% annually

### OTT/SVOD
- **Market size**: ~€600 million
- **Revenue/ARPU**: ~€12-15/month per service
- **Top players**: Netflix, Amazon Prime, Disney+, Zattoo, SRF Play
- **Subscriber growth rates**: ~8-10% annually

### Advertising
- **TV advertising revenue**: ~€700 million
- **AVOD/CTV ad spend**: ~€180 million
- **Growth forecasts**: ~2-3% annually
- **Programmatic adoption**: ~50%

## 8. Market Forecasts

- **Pay-TV household forecast (5-10 years)**: Stable to slight decline (~-1% annually)
- **OTT/SVOD penetration forecast**: Growth to ~75% by 2030
- **Broadband and FTTH growth**: Fiber-optic to reach ~50% by 2030
- **Economic forecast**: Moderate growth (~1.5% GDP annually)
- **Population forecast**: Growth to ~9.5 million by 2030
