# TV Platform Operator: M7 Skylink CZ

## 1. Executive Summary

- **Overview of the operator's business**: M7 Skylink CZ is a satellite pay-TV platform in the Czech Republic, operated by M7 Group. The platform provides premium content including sports, movies, series, and entertainment channels via satellite delivery. Skylink operates alongside freeSAT in the Czech Republic and Slovakia markets.

- **Key market position**: Major satellite pay-TV platform in Czech Republic, part of M7 Group with ~1.5 million customers in Czech Republic and Slovakia combined (Skylink and freeSAT)

- **Geographic footprint**: Czech Republic (and Slovakia via freeSAT)

- **Ownership structure**: Part of M7 Group (owned by Canal+ Group/Vivendi)

- **Overall growth/decline assessment**: Stable - M7 Group reported 3.6 million subscribers across European operations (2023). Czech/Slovak market shows ~1.5 million customers. European satellite market valued at USD 28,542 million in 2025 with 1% CAGR, indicating stable market environment.

- Competitive erosion from OTT: Competition from Netflix, Disney+, Amazon Prime Video, but also offers integrated services

### **Overall Assessment**

- **Operator Status:** Stable

- **Drivers Behind the Trend:** M7 Group reported 3.6 million subscribers across European operations (2023), with Czech/Slovak market showing ~1.5 million customers (Skylink and freeSAT combined). European satellite market valued at USD 28,542 million in 2025 with 1% CAGR indicates stable market environment. M7 Group ownership by Canal+ Group/Vivendi provides scale and content advantages. Czech pay-TV market showing growth with average monthly expenditure reaching CZK 424 in 2024 (first time exceeding CZK 400), indicating willingness to pay for premium services. However, market shift toward IPTV and cable (46% of households) vs terrestrial (41%) suggests satellite faces competition from modern platforms.
