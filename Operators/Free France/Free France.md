# TV Platform Operator: Free France (Iliad)

## 1. Executive Summary

- **Overview of the operator's business**: Free France (operated by Iliad Group) is a major IPTV service provider in France, offering TV content via IPTV (over broadband) integrated with Free's broadband and mobile services. Free is known for disruptive pricing and innovative services.

- **Key market position**: Major IPTV provider in France, ~6.7-8.6 million IPTV subscribers (varies by source/year), part of Iliad Group

- **Geographic footprint**: Nationwide coverage in France through Free's broadband network

- **Ownership structure**: Part of Iliad Group (French telecommunications company)

- **Overall growth/decline assessment**: Growth - Broadband subscribers 7.57 million (2024, +2.1%), ARPU €37.0/month (2024, +3.7% from €35.7). Fixed services revenue +9.4% (2024), Q1 2025 ARPU €36.3/month (+5.6% YoY), fixed services revenue +6.2%.

## 4. Growth Assessment

### 4.1 Market Position

- Ranking vs. competitors: Major IPTV provider in France, disruptive pricing strategy
- Strength in broadband, mobile, and TV: Strong in broadband and IPTV, mobile presence (Free Mobile)
- Differentiators: Disruptive pricing, innovative services, ultra-fast broadband, ARPU growth

### 4.2 Growth Indicators

- Subscriber trends across FTTH, broadband, TV, and mobile: Broadband subscribers growing (7.57M, +2.1%)
- ARPU trends: ARPU €37.0/month (+3.7% in 2024), €36.3/month (+5.6% YoY in Q1 2025)
- Network upgrades: Ultra-fast broadband expansion, fiber investments
- New service launches: Enhanced streaming capabilities, 4K/UHD content

### 4.3 Decline Indicators

- Cord-cutting pressure: Moderate pressure from OTT competitors
- Regulatory or pricing pressure: Standard regulatory environment
- Content rights losses: Standard content portfolio maintained
- Competitive erosion from OTT: Competition from Netflix, Disney+, Amazon Prime Video, but also offers integrated OTT services

### **Overall Assessment**

- **Operator Status:** Growth

- **Drivers Behind the Trend:** Strong broadband subscriber growth (7.57 million, +2.1% in 2024) with stable base in Q1 2025. ARPU growth demonstrates pricing power: €37.0/month (+3.7% in 2024), €36.3/month (+5.6% YoY in Q1 2025). Fixed services revenue growth +9.4% (2024) and +6.2% (Q1 2025) driven by ARPU growth averaging 4.5% over 2024. Strong growth in B2B services in Q1 2025. Disruptive pricing strategy and innovative services provide competitive advantages. ~6.7-8.6 million IPTV subscribers provides strong market position.
