# TV Platform Operator: SimpliTV SAT

## 1. Executive Summary

- **Overview of the operator's business**: SimpliTV SAT is a satellite pay-TV platform in Austria, operated by A1 Telekom Austria. The platform provides premium content including sports, movies, series, and entertainment channels via satellite delivery, integrated with A1's telecommunications services.

- **Key market position**: Satellite pay-TV platform in Austria, part of A1 Group (Austria's major telecommunications company)

- **Geographic footprint**: Nationwide coverage in Austria via satellite

- **Ownership structure**: Part of A1 Group (Telekom Austria AG)

- **Overall growth/decline assessment**: Stable - Part of A1 Group which reported 3.1% revenue growth in 2024 (€5.41 billion) and 5.1% EBITDA growth (exceeding €2 billion for first time). 2025 outlook anticipates 2-3% revenue growth.
