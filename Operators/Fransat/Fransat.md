# TV Platform Operator: Fransat

## 1. Executive Summary

- **Overview of the operator's business**: Fransat is a French satellite television service operated by Eutelsat, providing free-to-air channels to households with limited or no access to terrestrial digital television. The platform offers satellite delivery of French TV channels.

- **Key market position**: Satellite TV platform in France, operated by Eutelsat, providing free-to-air channel access

- **Geographic footprint**: Nationwide coverage in France via satellite (Eutelsat satellite system)

- **Ownership structure**: Operated by Eutelsat (French satellite operator)

- **Overall growth/decline assessment**: Stable - Part of Eutelsat's video services which contributed €608.2 million in FY2024-25 (6.5% decline attributed to market maturity). Eutelsat broadcasting ~2,400 free-to-air channels reaching over one billion viewers across Europe, Eurasia, Middle East, and Africa.
