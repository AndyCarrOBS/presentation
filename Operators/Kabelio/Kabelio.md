# TV Platform Operator: Kabelio

## 1. Executive Summary

- **Overview of the operator's business**: Kabelio is a satellite television service in Switzerland that offers a selection of cable TV channels via satellite. The platform provides access to various HD channels, including those previously available only through cable or in standard digital quality via satellite.

- **Key market position**: Satellite pay-TV platform in Switzerland, smaller market presence

- **Geographic footprint**: Nationwide coverage in Switzerland via satellite

- **Ownership structure**: Data not publicly disclosed

- **Overall growth/decline assessment**: Decline - Swiss pay-TV market shows only 0.7% of subscribers via satellite (2023), with 52% IPTV and 48% cable. Satellite TV market share declining, impacting providers like Kabelio. Overall pay-TV revenue stable around CHF 1.5 billion, but satellite share minimal.
