# TV Platform Operator: Bouygues Telecom

## 1. Executive Summary

- **Overview of the operator's business**: Bouygues Telecom is a major telecommunications operator in France, offering TV services via IPTV integrated with Bouygues Telecom's broadband, mobile, and fixed-line services. Part of Bouygues Group, it operates as a major player in the French telecom market.

- **Key market position**: Major telco operator in France, part of Bouygues Group

- **Geographic footprint**: Nationwide coverage in France

- **Ownership structure**: Part of Bouygues Group (French conglomerate)

- **Overall growth/decline assessment**: Growth - Revenue €7.8 billion (2024). Strong subscriber base: 18.38 million mobile plan subscribers and 5.27 million fixed-line customers (June 2025). Fixed services growth with 4.4 million FTTH customers by mid-2025.
