# TV Platform Operator: SFR

## 1. Executive Summary

- **Overview of the operator's business**: SFR is a major telecommunications operator in France, offering TV services via IPTV integrated with SFR's broadband, mobile, and fixed-line services. SFR is part of Altice France and operates as a major player in the French telecom market.

- **Key market position**: Major telco operator in France, part of Altice France

- **Geographic footprint**: Nationwide coverage in France

- **Ownership structure**: Part of Altice France (owned by Patrick Drahi)

- **Overall growth/decline assessment**: Challenged - Revenue €10.08 billion (2024, -5.6% YoY), EBITDA €3.351 billion. Significant subscriber losses (1.3 million in 2024). Debt restructuring agreement reached in February 2025, reducing debt from €24 billion to €15 billion.

## 4. Growth Assessment

### 4.1 Market Position

- Ranking vs. competitors: Major telco operator in France, but facing challenges
- Strength in broadband, mobile, and TV: Strong in all three segments, but subscriber losses
- Differentiators: Multi-play bundles, SFR brand

### 4.2 Growth Indicators

- Subscriber trends across FTTH, broadband, TV, and mobile: Significant losses (1.3 million in 2024)
- ARPU trends: Data not publicly disclosed
- Network upgrades: Fiber expansion ongoing
- New service launches: Enhanced streaming capabilities, service improvements

### 4.3 Decline Indicators

- Cord-cutting pressure: Significant pressure from competitors
- Regulatory or pricing pressure: Competitive market pressure
- Content rights losses: Standard content portfolio maintained
- Competitive erosion from OTT: Competition from Netflix, Disney+, Amazon Prime Video, Orange, Free

### **Overall Assessment**

- **Operator Status:** Decline / Challenged

- **Drivers Behind the Trend:** Revenue declined 5.6% in 2024 (€10.08 billion) with significant subscriber losses (1.3 million in 2024) across services. EBITDA €3.351 billion provides some financial stability. Debt restructuring agreement reached in February 2025, reducing debt from €24 billion to €15 billion, providing financial relief. However, subscriber losses and revenue decline indicate competitive challenges. Altice France rejected acquisition offers from Bouygues, Orange, and Iliad Group in October 2025 (€17 billion), indicating commitment to turnaround strategy.
