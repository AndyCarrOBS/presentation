# TV Platform Operator: Canal+ France

## 1. Executive Summary

- **Overview of the operator's business**: Canal+ France is a major satellite pay-TV platform in France, offering premium sports content, movies, series, and entertainment channels. Part of Canal+ Group (Vivendi), it is the largest pay-TV platform in France and the flagship market for Canal+ Group.

- **Key market position**: #1 Pay-TV provider in France, ~4.7-9.1 million subscribers (varies by reporting method), largest Canal+ Group market

- **Geographic footprint**: Nationwide coverage in France via satellite

- **Ownership structure**: Part of Canal+ Group (Vivendi-owned French media company)

- **Overall growth/decline assessment**: Challenged - Revenue growth 3.6% in 2024 (€6.45 billion), but 2025 outlook shows revenue decline expected due to C8 free-to-air broadcast cessation and Disney contract loss. H1 2025 revenue -3.3% (€3.09 billion). Total customer base 26.9 million (2024), D2C base +2%.

### 4.3 Decline Indicators

- Cord-cutting pressure: Moderate pressure from OTT competitors
- Regulatory or pricing pressure: C8 free-to-air broadcast cessation impacting revenue
- Content rights losses: Lost Disney contract impacting 2025 revenue
- Competitive erosion from OTT: Competition from Netflix, Disney+, Amazon Prime Video, but also operates streaming services

### **Overall Assessment**

- **Operator Status:** Challenged / Decline

- **Drivers Behind the Trend:** Revenue growth 3.6% in 2024 (€6.45 billion) driven by high-value D2C subscriptions, exclusive content partnerships, and live premium sports events. Total customer base 26.9 million (2024) with net increase of 118,000 subscribers, D2C base +2%. Company maintains confidence in achieving full-year earnings target of ~€515 million, but revenue growth expected to be flat or negative in 2025.
