# TV Platform Operator: Canal+ Poland (nc+)

## 1. Executive Summary

- **Overview of the operator's business**: Canal+ Poland (operating as nc+) is a major satellite pay-TV platform in Poland, offering premium sports content, movies, series, and entertainment channels. Part of Canal+ Group, it is the second-largest market for Canal+ Group after France.

- **Key market position**: #2 Pay-TV provider in Poland, ~2.7 million subscribers (August 2025), second-largest Canal+ Group market after France

- **Geographic footprint**: Nationwide coverage in Poland via satellite

- **Ownership structure**: Part of Canal+ Group (Vivendi-owned French media company)

- **Overall growth/decline assessment**: Growth - Strong subscriber base (~2.7 million), double-digit pay-TV revenue growth in 2024 driven by increased subscriptions, higher advertising revenue, and favorable currency exchange rates.
