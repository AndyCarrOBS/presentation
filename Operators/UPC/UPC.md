# TV Platform Operator: UPC Switzerland (Sunrise UPC)

## 1. Executive Summary

- **Overview of the operator's business**: UPC Switzerland, now part of Sunrise UPC following merger, is a major cable and telecommunications operator in Switzerland, offering TV, broadband internet, mobile, and fixed-line services. The company operates cable and IPTV services across Switzerland.

- **Key market position**: Major cable/telco operator in Switzerland, part of Sunrise UPC, expected 32% market share by 2029

- **Geographic footprint**: Nationwide coverage in Switzerland

- **Ownership structure**: Part of Sunrise UPC (merged entity)

- **Overall growth/decline assessment**: Stable - Revenue CHF 3,018.0 million in 2024 (-0.6%), Adjusted EBITDAaL CHF 1,029.9 million (+0.7%). Q1 2025 revenue CHF 1,453.7 million (-2.1%), but EBITDAaL +1.1%. Net customer growth with 159,000 new mobile postpaid and 31,000 new internet subscriptions in 2024.
