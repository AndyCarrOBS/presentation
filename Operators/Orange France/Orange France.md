# TV Platform Operator: Orange France

## 1. Executive Summary

- **Overview of the operator's business**: Orange France is a major IPTV service provider in France, offering TV content via IPTV (over broadband) integrated with Orange's broadband, mobile, and fixed-line services. Orange is France's largest telecommunications operator.

- **Key market position**: #1 IPTV provider in France, ~7.6-11.6 million IPTV subscribers (varies by source/year), part of France's largest telco

- **Geographic footprint**: Nationwide coverage in France through Orange's extensive broadband network

- **Ownership structure**: Part of Orange Group (French telecommunications company)

- **Overall growth/decline assessment**: Growth - Strong fiber customer growth (10 million fiber customers by Q3 2025), convergent ARPU €77.8 (+€3.0 YoY in Q1 2025). Retail services excluding PSTN +1.5% YoY in Q1 2025. Domestic revenue -3.7% YoY in Q3 2025 due to copper decommissioning.

## 4. Growth Assessment

### 4.1 Market Position

- Ranking vs. competitors: #1 IPTV provider in France, #1 telecommunications provider
- Strength in broadband, mobile, and TV: Strong in all three segments
- Differentiators: Integrated convergent offerings, extensive fiber network, Orange brand, convergent ARPU €77.8/month

### 4.2 Growth Indicators

- Subscriber trends across FTTH, broadband, TV, and mobile: Strong fiber growth (10 million milestone, 282,000 added Q1 2025)
- ARPU trends: Convergent ARPU €77.8/month (+€3.0 YoY in Q1 2025)
- Network upgrades: Significant fiber expansion, 10 million fiber customers milestone
- New service launches: Enhanced streaming capabilities, 4K/UHD content, convergent services

### 4.3 Decline Indicators

- Cord-cutting pressure: Moderate pressure from OTT competitors
- Regulatory or pricing pressure: Standard regulatory environment
- Content rights losses: Standard content portfolio maintained
- Competitive erosion from OTT: Competition from Netflix, Disney+, Amazon Prime Video, but also offers integrated OTT services

### **Overall Assessment**

- **Operator Status:** Growth

- **Drivers Behind the Trend:** Strong fiber customer growth with 10 million fiber customers milestone reached in Q3 2025 and 282,000 fiber customers added in Q1 2025. Convergent ARPU €77.8/month (+€3.0 YoY in Q1 2025) demonstrates strong multi-play performance. Retail services excluding PSTN +1.5% YoY in Q1 2025 driven by fiber and convergence services. Domestic revenue -3.7% YoY in Q3 2025 primarily due to copper decommissioning (ongoing network modernization), not service decline. #1 IPTV provider position with ~7.6-11.6 million IPTV subscribers provides strong market position.
